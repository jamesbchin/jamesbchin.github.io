Week 17.1


"Why won't you let go of me?"

"I am looking for adventure, and you are my ticket to the great unknown!"

"You are so annoying, you know that?"

"Adventure is for those brave enough to do whatever is necessary!"

Percy sighed, but he could do nothing about his fate, and thus kept on driving. He accidentally picked up a hitchhiker and, well, has been unable to get rid of him.

It's a funny story though, really, how Percy managed to accidentally pick up this adventurous child. A story for another time, but a story nonetheless.
