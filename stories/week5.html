Week 5


Every writer gets to write this story only once. The story about how they have no idea what to write. The "Writer's Block" story. The story where the writer talks about how they have so many ideas, but also none. The science fiction story, the fantasy, maybe even a p---


"Hey James! What are you up to?"

A girl comes walking down the hall, seeing her friend sitting at a random desk in the computer science building. She's carrying her large backpack, full of all the textbooks and notebooks required for her first year at college. One day she would be like James, carrying one small little bag with just a folder and a tablet.

"Nothing nothing, just trying to write my story for the week."

"Oh what, you write a story every week?"

James sits back in his chair, stretching his arms up towards the ceiling. He takes off his mask so he can take a sip of his Fanta, the bottle he just bought from the vending machine around the corner.

"Well, I try to. I missed last week unfortunately."

He opens the bottle of Fanta and takes a sip. He can feel his teeth rotting away from the sugar in his drink. He lifts up the bottle and takes a look at the Nutrition Facts. 73 grams.

"Hey look, they're leaving the classroom. We can go in and print the posters now."
