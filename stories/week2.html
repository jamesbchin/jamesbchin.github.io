Trip to City Hall

You fiddle.
You look at the map.
You step on that forbidden yellow line,
and look over the edge.
"Where is the train?"

Read a book. Solve a puzzle.
Skim a newspaper.

A light appears, far away but slowly
coming
nearer.

You jump. You're excited!
It has finally
arrived.

The doors open. You run inside.
"Stand clear for the closing doors please."
Two tones. Clunk.
You are on your way.

77th. Bay Ridge Av. 59th.
"We are being delayed because of train traffic ahead of us"
"We are being held momentarily by the train's dispatcher."
"We apologize for any inconvenience"

53rd. 45th. 36th.
"This train will now be running on the
N Line"

You rest your head.
Just for a little while.
Fourth rung.
