Week 14


Is it better to know that something is slipping away, or to never know of its existence. Blissful ignorance or unfortunate truth.

Unfortunately, some of us don't have the luxury of choice.

It's like an addiction. It is an addiction. Just one more time, just one more time. A never ending question to answer. Can I have just one more?

Every spell, every little bit of magic, takes time off your life. Every tiny little itsy bitsy taste of the art, brings you that much closer to death. Some try to calculate how much magic they have left. Some try to ration it, some have more of a #yolo approach to life. And some don't do the math. Some just live their lives, using magic only when absolutely necessary.

Magic users get exactly 150 years to live. Or so we think. No magic user has ever lived that long, most averaging a death at around 60 or so. The most skilled of us all are among the youngest, only practicing their power when absolutely necessary, performing the most beautifully crafted spells. They spend their time studying, thinking, simulating. Practice is a curse.
