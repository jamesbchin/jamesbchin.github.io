I would like to thank The Harry Chin Foundation of Vancouver, Canada who extended me the privilege of setting up the Zhong Shan Chen Wenlong Genealogy on the Web. 陳文龍 Chen Wenlong was the third son of Chen Mo. He was missing in action during the struggle between the Song and the Yuan forces. One son, 天覺 Tiānjué, managed to survive in the 香山 Xiāng Shān (now 中山 Zhōng Shān) district in Guangdong Province.
