My sixth younger brother, Hũ, moved to Gū Göng 古岡 (Gǔgāng), Shïm Chông Chān Wän 深涌滻灣 (Shēnyǒngchǎnwān), Chẽl Lẽin Āi Ngòk 潮連大岳(Cháoliándàyuè).

Sì 視 (Shì) and Hëng 聽 (Tīng) moved from Gū Göng 古岡 (Gǔgāng) to various places in Lẽin Tûn 淩村 (Língcūn).
Ngũn 言 (Yán) lived in various places in Gū Göng 古岡 (Gǔgāng), Shïm Chông Chān Wän 深涌滻灣 (Shēnyǒngchǎnwān), Chẽl Lẽin Āi Ngòk 潮連大岳(Cháoliándàyuè).
