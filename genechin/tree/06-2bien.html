Our sixth generation Great Ancestor Bēik Yïn fathered Ēo Xiü and Ēo Tẽng. Our seventh generation Great Ancestor Ēo Xiü fathered Dū Yẽn, Dū Mũn and Dū Chëng. Ēo Xiü's younger brother, Ēo Tẽng, did not have any sons, so he gave his third son, Dū Chëng, to his younger brother to raise as his own son.
