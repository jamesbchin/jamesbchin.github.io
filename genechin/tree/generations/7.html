THE SEVENTH GENERATION

斗秀 Ēo Xiü was the first son of 必恩 Bēik Yïn. He married a woman from the Vü 胡 (Hú) family. His tomb was in Ngùk Vâi 玉槐 (Yùhuái) facing south. He had three sons: 子賢 Dū Yẽn, 文 Dū Mũn, and 子昌 Dū Chëng. [子昌 Dū Chëng was given to 斗祥 Ēo Tẽng to raise as his own son.]

斗祥 Ēo Tẽng was the second son of 必恩 Bēik Yïn. He married a woman from the Fũng 馮 (Féng) family. His tomb was in Tëin Lũng Sān Ēng 青龍山巔 (Qīnglóngshāndiān). His son, 子昌 Dū Chëng, [was given by his older brother 斗秀 Ēo Xiü.]
