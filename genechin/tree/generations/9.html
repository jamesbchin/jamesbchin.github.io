THE NINTH GENERATION

良佑 Lẽng Yiù was the first son of 子賢 Dū Yẽn. He married a woman from the Yï 余 (Yú) family. His tomb was in Bön Gëin 半逕 (Bànjìng). The first born of 召南 Sèl Nãm, 瑞應 Xuì Yëin, was given to 良佑 Lẽng Yiù to raise as his own son.

召南 Sèl Nãm was the second son of 子賢 Dū Yẽn. He married a woman from the Gäo 高 (Gāo) family. His tomb was in Yẽn Böi 焉背 (Yānbēi). He had three sons. The first son of 召南 Sèl Nãm, 瑞應 Xuì Yëin, was given to 良佑 Lẽng Yiù to raise as his own son. The second son was 瑞煊 Xuì Xün and the third son was 瑞煇 Xuì Fï.

安南 Ön Nãm was the third son of 子賢 Dū Yẽn. He married a woman from the Föng 鄺 (Kuàng) family. His tomb was in Ngùk Vâi 玉槐 (Yùhuái). The second son of 定南 Èin Nãm, 瑞珪 Xuì Gï, was given to 安南 Ön Nãm to raise as his own son.

定南 Èin Nãm was the fourth son of 子賢 Dū Yẽn. He was born in 1441 C.E. He died on the twelfth day of the twelfth moon in the year 1499 C.E. at the age of 58. He married a woman from the Jï 朱 (Zhū) clan. Her tomb was in Yùhuái 玉槐. The landscape of the tomb, which faces south, is in the shape of a big seat for a fairy. His second wife was from the Lëng 梁 (Liáng) clan. Her tomb was in Võng Hẽin 黃田 (Huángtián). The landscape of the tomb, which faces west, is in the shape of a snake. He had three sons: 瑞徵 Xuì Jëin, 瑞珪 Xuì Gï, and 瑞玉 Xuì Ngùk.
