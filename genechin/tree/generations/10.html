THE TENTH GENERATION

瑞應 Xuì Yëin was the first son of 召南 Sèl Nãm. He was given to his older uncle, 良佑 Lẽng Yiù, to be raised. He married a woman from the Jèng 鄭 (Zhèng) family. He had one son, 惠雄 Fì Hũng.

瑞煊 Xuì Xün was the second son of 召南 Sèl Nãm. He married a woman from the Yïn 殷 (Yīn)family. His tomb was in Hoi Pein 開平 (Kāipíng). He had one son, 遠迪 Yōn Èik.

瑞煇 Xuì Fï was the third son of 召南 Sèl Nãm. He married a woman from the Yëin 英 (Yīng) family. He had one son, 戊長 Mù Jēng.

瑞徵 Xuì Jëin was the first son of 定南 Èin Nãm. He was born in 1466 C.E. At the age of 48 he married a woman from the Jï 朱 (Zhū) family. His tomb was in Võng Tûn Gēo Sän Ēng 潢村狗山巔 (Huángcūngǒushāndiān) in the shape of a dragon facing ENE 3/4N or 60 degrees. He had one son, 雲穀 Vũn Gūk.

瑞珪 Xuì Gï was the second son of 定南 Èin Nãm. He was given to his older uncle, 安南 Ön Nãm, to be raised as his own son. He married a woman from the Jëng 張 (Zhāng) family. His tomb was in Võng Nãi Hõng 黃坭塘 (Huángnítáng). He had two sons, 應良 Yëin Lẽng and 應鸞 Yëin Lũn.

瑞玉 Xuì Ngùk was the third son of 定南 Èin Nãm. He married a woman from the Föng 鄺 (Kuàng) family. He had two sons, 君剴 Gün Köi and 君傑 Gün Gèik.

觀生 Gön Säng was the first son of 受南 Siù Nãm. He married a woman from the Võng 黃 (Huáng) family. His tomb was in Mâ Ùng 馬垌 (Mǎdòng) in the shape of a dog facing SSW 3/4W or 210 degrees. He had two sons, 君俊 Gün Dün and 君勇 Gün Yûng.

觀綱 Gön Göng was the second son of 受南 Siù Nãm. He married a woman from the Jüng 鐘 (Zhōng) family. He had one son, 君養 Gün Yëng.
