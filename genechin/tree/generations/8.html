THE EIGHTH GENERATION

子賢 Dū Yẽn was the first son of 斗秀 Ēo Xiü. His tomb was in Ngùk Vâi 玉槐 (Yùhuái) in the shape of a sitting fairy facing south. His wife was from the Võng 黃 (Huáng) family. Her tomb was in Hõ Jiü 河州 (Hézhōu) facing west. 子賢 Dū Yẽn had five sons: 良佑 Lẽng Yiù, 召南 Sèl Nãm, 安南 Ön Nãm, 定南 Èin Nãm, and 受南 Siù Nãm.

子文 Dū Mũn was the second son of 斗秀 Ēo Xiü. His wife was from the Xün 孫 (Sūn) family.

子昌 Dū Chëng was the third son of 斗秀 Ēo Xiü. He was given to 斗祥 Ēo Tẽng to raise as his own son. His wife was from the Diù 鄒 (Zōu) family.
