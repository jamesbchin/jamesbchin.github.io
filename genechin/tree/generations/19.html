THE NINETEENTH GENERATION

朝賓 Chẽl Bïn was the first son of 伯位 Bâk Vì. He married a woman from the Yï 余 (Yú) family. She bore him one son named 宗維 Düng Vĩ. After Yï 余 (Yú) died, 朝賓 Chẽl Bïn married a woman from the Lī 李 (Lǐ) family. The tomb of 朝賓 Chẽl Bïn was in Nãm Bëin Hǎng 南邊坑 (Nánbiānkēng) in the shape of a phoenix facing west. Yï 余 (Yú) was buried next to her husband. The tomb of Lī 李 (Lǐ) was in Gēo Sän 狗山 (Gǒushān).

朝苧 Chẽl Chuî was the second son of 伯位 Bâk Vì.

朝宗 Chẽl Düng was the third son of 伯位 Bâk Vì. His tomb was in Ài Hǎng Hēo 大坑口 (Daikēngkǒu) with the shape of an old snake facing north. She had three sons, 瑞晃 Xuì Fōng, 瑞暹 Xuì Tëm, and 瑞昊 Xuì Hâo.
