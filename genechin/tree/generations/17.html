THE SEVENTEENTH GENERATION

耀馨 Yèl Hëng was the second son of 爵君 Dēk Gün. He married a woman from the Võng 黃 (Huáng) family. His tomb was in this villages's Lèin Hẽin Höt 另挺喝 (Lìngtǐnghē) in the shape of an old snake. He had three sons, 伯超 Bâk Chël (Bóchāo), 伯位 Bâk Vì (Bówèi), and 伯達 Bâk Àt (Bódá).

The manuscript wrote Lèin 另 with the insect 虫 radical on the left and 另 on the right, [虫另].
