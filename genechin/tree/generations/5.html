THE FIFTH GENERATION

元隆 Ngũn Lũng was 希堯 Hï Ngẽl's second son. He married a woman from the Däng 曾 Céng family. His tomb was in Äp Nā Hǎng 鴨乸坑 (Yānǎkēng).

應隆 Yëin Lũng was 希堯 Hï Ngẽl's third son. He married a woman from the Yï 余 (Yú) family. He had two sons: 必榮 Bēik Vẽin and 必恩 Bēik Yïn. His tomb was in Hoi Pein 開平 (Kāipíng) in the shape of a dragon facing east. He had another tomb in Xäm Gâp 三合 (Sānhé) in the shape of a child reciting from a book facing ENE 3/4N or 60 degrees. In mid-spring of [1984] his tomb in Xäm Gâp 三合 (Sānhé) was renovated.

宗義 Düng Ngì was 希堯 Hï Ngẽl's fourth son. He married a woman from the Jèng 鄭 (Zhèng) family.
