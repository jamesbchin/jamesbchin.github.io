THE FOURTH GENERATION

希堯 Hï Ngẽl was the first born of 仲真 Jùng Jïn. He married a woman from the Mâ 馬 (Mǎ) family. His tomb was in Võng Tûn - Sïm Lũng Bāo 潢村 - 深龍寶 (Huángcūn - Shēnlóngbǎo) in the shape of a duck facing ENE 3/4N or 60 degrees. He had four sons: 慶隆 Hëin Lũng, 元隆 Ngũn Lũng, 應隆 Yëin Lũng, and 宗義 Düng Ngì.
