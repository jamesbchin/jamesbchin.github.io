THE SECOND GENERATION

文詵 Mũn Xëin was our second generation Grand Older Uncle. He received the title of Eleventh Lõng 郎 [Láng]. He was the the first son of 謨翁 Mũ Yǔng. He married [in 1201 C.E.] a woman from the Jï 朱 (Zhū) family. Her tomb was in Fāt Jūk Lẽng Bëin Lěng 法竹梁邊嶺 (Fǎzhúliángbiānlǐng).His second wife was from the Jüng 鐘 (Zhōng) clan. His third wife was from the Mâ 馬 (Mǎ) family. Their tombs were in 沖金石焦龍山 (Chōngjīnshíjiāolóngshān). His fourth wife was from the Võng 黃 (Huáng) family. She gave birth to five sons: 有仁 Yiû Ngĩn, 有義 Yiû Ngì, 有禮 Yiû Lî, 有智 Yiû Jï, and 有信 Yiû Xïn. His tomb was in Lũng Suī Ù 瀧水都 (Lóngshuǐdū).

文振 Mũn Jīn was our second generation Grand Older Uncle. He received the title of Twelfth Lõng 郎 [Láng]. He was the the second son of 謨翁 Mũ Yǔng. His wife came from the Lëng 梁 (Liáng) family. Her tomb was in Chüng Vũn Gāp Gäo Tǔn 沖雲甲高村 (Chōngyúnjiǎgāocūn). His tomb was in Lũng Suī Ù 瀧水都 (Lóngshuǐdū).

文龍 Mũn Lũng was our second generation Grand Older Uncle. He received the title of Thirteenth Lõng 郎 [Láng]. He was the the third son of 謨翁 Mũ Yǔng. His wife came from the M 伍 (Wǔ) family. During the struggle between the Xüng 宋 (Sòng) and Ngũn 元 (Yuán) forces he was lost. He either joined the army or just wandered off somewhere.

文起 Mũn Hī was our second generation Grand Ancestor. He received the title of Fourteenth Lõng 郎 [Láng]. He was the the fourth son of 謨翁 Mũ Yǔng. He married [in 1201 C.E.] a woman from the Jiü 周 (Zhōu) family. Tehy had four sons, 仲典Jùng Ēin, 仲誥 Jùng Gäo, 仲訓 Jùng Fün, and 仲真 Jùng Jïn. Her tomb was in Höi Yòn Hôk Dū Dēik 開縣鶴子節(Kāixiànhèzǐjié). His second wife was from the Mâ 馬 (Mǎ) family. Her tomb was in Xëin Ùng Xēl Yõn Sän 仙洞小員山 (Xiāndòngxiǎoyuánshān). His third wife was from the Yẽng 楊 (Yáng) family. Her tomb was in Xēl Yõn Sän 小員山 (Xiǎoyuánshān). [In 1254 C.E.] her grave site was dugged up by grave robbers and the Yẽng 楊 (Yáng) family took possession of the grave site. 文起 Mũn Hī's tomb was in Lũng Suī Ù 瀧水都 (Lóngshuǐdū).
