THE EIGHTEENTH GENERATION

伯位 Bâk Vì was the second son of 耀馨 Yèl Hëng. His tomb was in Jēik Nuī Vön 織女灣 (Zhīnǚwān) in the shape of a beautiful girl tossing her comb away facing east. He was buried next to 耀章 Yèl Jëng (his uncle from the 仲倫 Jùng Lũn branch) and 伯徽 Bâk Fï (his cousin). He married a woman from the Hâm 譚 (Tán) family. Her tomb was in Päo Bǔ Ì Mèin Höt 泡步地面喝 (Pàobù Dìmiànhē) in the shape of a cat facing north. He had three sons, 朝賓 Chẽl Bïn, 朝苧 Chẽl Chuî, and 朝宗 Chẽl Düng.
