THE THIRTEENTH GENERATION

如璋 Nguĩ Jëng was the first son of 仲乾 Jùng Kẽin. He married a woman from the Jï 朱 (Zhū) family. He had two sons, 廷富 Hẽin Fü and 廷貴 Hẽin Gï.

善道 Sên Ào was the son of 仲倫 Jùng Lũn. His style name was Nî Gëin 汝敬 (Rǔjìng). His generation name was Sēl Ngãi 少崖 (Shǎoyá). He left Xïn Nẽin Yǒn 新寧縣 (Xīnnìngxiàn) to teach in Göng Xäi Güng Jiü Fū Xuì Gïm Yǒn 江西贑州府瑞金縣 (Jiāngxī Gànzhōufǔ Ruìjīnxiàn). He was promoted to another teaching position in Sän Üng Lû Fū 山東魯府 (Shāndōnglǔfǔ). His tomb was in Xäm Gâp Huǐ Hèo 三合墟後 (Sānhéxūhòu) in the shape of a beautiful girl looking at a mirror. His wife was from the Hâm 譚 (Tán) family. He had two sons, 言昌 Ngũn Chëng and 言衷 Ngũn Chüng.
