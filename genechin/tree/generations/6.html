THE SIXTH GENERATION

必榮 Bēik Vẽin was the first son of 應隆 Yëin Lũng. He married a woman from the Hâm 譚 (Tán) family.

必恩 Bēik Yïn was the second son of 應隆 Yëin Lũng. His tomb was in Jüng Xïm Pẽin 中心坪 (Zhōngxīnpíng) in the shape of a snake. He married a woman from the M 吳 (Wú) family. Her tomb was in Yïn Pẽin Sïm Hǎng 恩平深坑 (Ēnpíngshēnkēng) in the shape of a person facing ENE 3/4N or 60 degrees. They had two sons: 斗秀 Ēo Xiü and 斗祥 Ēo Tẽng.
