The entire Chen/Chin/Chan/Tan Clan 陳氏 are descendents of Emperor Shùndì 舜帝. He ascended the throne in 2255 BCE. His Dynastic Appellation was Yudishun (Chinese: 虞帝舜; pinyin: Yúdìshùn; Hoisanva: Nguĩ Äi Sün). His Personal Appellation was Youyu (Chinese:有虞; pinyin: Yǒuyú; Hoisanva: Yiû Nguĩ) . Thus his full title was Yudishun Youyu (Chinese: 虞帝舜有虞; pinyin: Yúdìshùn Yǒuyú; Hoisanva: Nguĩ Äi Sün Yiû Nguĩ).
