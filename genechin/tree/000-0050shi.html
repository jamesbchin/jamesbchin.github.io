Shigong (traditinal Chinese: 實公; simplified Chinese: 实公; pinyin: Shígōng; Hoisanva: Sìt Güng) was born many generations from Mangong. His courtesy name was Daqiu (Chinese: 大邱; pinyin: Dàqiu; Hoisanva: Ài Hiü). He was responsible for protecting Yingchuanshijun (traditional Chinese: 穎川始郡; simplified Chinese: 颖川始郡; pinyin: Yingchuanshijùn; Hoisanva: Vêin Chün Chi Gùn).
