Dialogue
1 A: Hāo lā-mä?
B: Hāo hāo. Nï nēh?
A: Hāo hāo.		 A: How are you?
B: Fine. How are you?
A: Fine.
2 A: Kôi jï hâi m˜-hâi fūn-bīt?
B: Hâi, kôi jï hâi fūn-bīt.		 A: Is this a piece of chalk?
B: Yes, it is a piece of chalk.
3 A: Nêin jï hâi m˜-hâi yõn-bīt?
B: M˜-hâi, nêin jï m˜-hâi yõn-bīt.		 A: Is that a pencil?
B: No, that is not a pencil.
4 A: Kôi yīt bù hâi m˜-hâi tāo-gāo-bù/?
B: Hâi, kôi yīt bù hâi tāo-gāo-bù/		 A: Is this a writing tablet?
B: Yes, it is a writing tablet.
5 A: Nêin yīt jëng hâi m˜-hâi bäo-jī?
B: M˜-hâi, nêin yīt jëng m˜-hâi bäo-jī.		 A: Is that a newspaper?
B: No, that is not a newspaper.
6 A: Kôi bōn hâi dù-eīn m˜-hâi?
B: Hâi, kôi bōn hâi dù-eīn.		 A: Is this a dictionary?
B: Yes, this is a dictionary.
7

A: Nêin jï hâi fūn-bīt m˜-hâi?
B: M˜-hâi, nêin jï m˜-hâi fūn-bīt.

 A: Is that a piece of chalk?
B: No, that is not a piece of chalk.
8

A: Kôi yīt jï hâi yõn-bīt m˜-hâi?
B: Hâi, kôi yīt jï hâi yõn-bīt.

 A: Is this a pencil?
B: Yes, this is a pencil.
9 A: Nêin yīt bōn hâi tāo-gāo-bù/ m˜-hâi?
B: M˜-hâi, nêin yīt bōn m˜-hâi tāo-gāo-bù/.		 A: Is that a writing tablet?
B: No, that is not a writing tablet.
10 A: Hèin-dāo gëin.
B: Hèin-dāo döi gëin.		 A: See you tomorrow.
B: See you tomorrow again.
