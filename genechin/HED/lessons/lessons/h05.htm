Dialogue
1 A: Nï hāo mä?
B: Ngöi hāo, nï nēh?
A: Ngöi hāo hāo.

A: How are you?
B: I'm fine. And you?
A: I'm fine, too.

2 A: Kôi yīt gän hâi m˜-hâi hòk-hào?
B: Hâi, kôi yīt gän hâi hòk-hào?		 A: Is this a school?
B: Yes, it is.
3 A: Nêin yīt gän hòk-hào yiü mäo bän-fông/ ä?
B: Yiü, nêin yīt gän hòk-hào yiü bän-fông/. 		A: Does that school have classrooms?
B: Yes, that school has classrooms.
4 A: Kôi yīt gän bän-fông/ yiü mäo xëin-säng ä?
B: Mäo, kôi yīt gän bän-fông/ mäo xëin-säng.		 A: Does this classroom have a teacher?
B: No, this classroom does not have a teacher.
5 A: Nêin yīt gän bän-fông/ yiü mäo hāk-bān ä?
B: Yiü, nêin yīt gän bän-fông/ yiü hāk-bān.		 A: Does that classroom have a blackboard?
B: Yes, that classroom has a blackboard.
6 A: Kôi yīt gän bän-fông/ yiü hòk-säng mäo nēh?
B: Mäo, kôi yīt gän bän-fông/ mäo hòk-säng.		 A: Does this classroom have students?
B: No, this classroom does not have any students.
7 A: Nêin yīt gän ūk yiü tẽng mäo nēh?
B: Yiü, nêin yīt gän ūk yiü tẽng.		 A: Does that house have a wall?
B: Yes, that house has a wall.
8 A: Kôi yīt gän ūk yiü töng mä?
B: Mäo, kôi yīt gän ūk mäo töng.		 A: Does this house have windows?
B: No, this house does not have windows.
9 A: Nêin yīt gän ūk yiü mõn mä?
B: Yiü, nêin yīt gän ūk yiü mõn.		 A: Does that house have doors?
B: Yes, that house has doors.
10 A: M˜-göi, m˜-göi.
B: M˜-sōi m˜-göi.		 A: Thank you.
B: No need to thank me.
