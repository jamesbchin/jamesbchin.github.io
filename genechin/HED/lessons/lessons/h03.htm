Lesson 3

Note: Lesson 3 and all following lessons are adopted from the lessons in the Army Language Institute (ALI). These lessons are now in the Public Domain. The Romanization used by the ALI has been modernized and explained in the Introduction. Obvious errors in ALI have been corrected. Dialogues were translated into English by Gene M. Chin.

Words
1 âng chair
2 bīt pen
3 bù/ notebook, exercise book
4 dāo-sĩn good morning
5 dàp-jï/ magazine
6 döi-gëin good-bye! to see you again!
7 hâi is, are, am, were, was
8 hôi/ table, desk
9 jī paper
10 kôi nāi these, this
11 kôi yīt this one
12 kûng nāi those, that
13 kûng yīt that one
14 mōt what? what kind?
15 nâi nāi which?
16 nêin nāi those, that
17 nêin yīt that one
18 sï book
19 và-bäo/ magazine
20 yī chair

 

Dialogue
1 A: Dāo-sĩn, dāo-sĩn.
B: Dāo-sĩn, dāo-sĩn.		 A: Good morning.
B: Good morning.
2 A: Kôi nāi hâi mōt-yëh?
B: Kôi nāi hâi sï
A: What are these (things)?
B: These are books.
3 A: Nêin nāi hâi mōt-yëh?
B: Nêin nāi hâi bīt.
A: What are those (things)?
B: Those are pens.
4 A: Kôi nāi hâi mōt?
B: Kôi nāi hâi bù/. 		A: What are these?
B: These are notebooks.
5 A: Nêin nāi hâi mōt?
B: Nêin nāi hâi âng. 		A: What are those?
B: Those are chairs.
6 A: Nâi nāi hâi jī?
B: Nêin nāi hâi jī? 		A: Which are paper?
B: Those are paper.
7 A: Nâi nāi hâi hôi/?
B: Kôi nāi hâi hôi/? 		A: Which are tables?
B: These are tables.
8 A: Kôi nāi hâi mōt?
B: Kôi nāi hâi dàp-jï/. 		A: What are these?
B: These are magazines.
9 A: Döi-gëin, döi-gëin.
B: Döi-gëin, döi-gëin. 		A: Good bye.
B: Good bye.
