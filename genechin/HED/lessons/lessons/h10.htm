Lesson 10

Words
1 āi short in height
2 ài big, large, old in age
3 äk-dài too, intensive degree
4 äk-kãn to have leisure time
5 dâp-mân/ yesterday
6 ēin-dëm quite
7 fï-sẽng jï extremely, extraordinary
8 fĩ fat, bulky, greasy
9 gäo tall, high, superior
10 gëo tired
11 gī quite, a few, several, how?
12 gïm-mân, gïm-ngìt today
13 gìn-lõi/ recently, lately
14 gök-āk to feel
15 hāo sōng very nice
16 hëin-hï weather
17 läng cold, chilly
18 lāo-pû wife
19 lẽng cool
20 mäk-kãn to be busy
21 mõng busy
22 ngèik hot, warm, heat
23 nuī-ngĩn/ woman, female, wife
24 sëo skinny, thin
25 tẽin-ngìt/ day before yesterday
26 lū-yä final particle
27 xäi small, little, young in age
28 xäm-mīn-dōi child, son

 

Dialogue
1 A: Gī-hâo/ yä, gìn-lõi/ mõng mä?
B: Gìn-lõi/ hāo mõng, nï nēh?
A: Ngöi gìn-lõi/ m~-mõng.		 A: How is it? Have you not been busy lately?
B: I have been very busy lately. How about you?
A: I have not been busy lately.
2 A: Gïm-mân göi hëin-hï hāo m~-hāo ä?
B: Hāo, gïm-mân göi hëin-hï hāo hāo.		 A: How is the weather today?
B: Good, today's weather is very good.
3 A: Dâp-mân/ ngèik m~-ngèik nēh?
B: Ngèik, dâp-mân/ hāo ngèik.		 A: Was it hot yesterday?
B: It was very hot yesterday.
4 A: Tẽin-ngìt/ lẽng m~-lẽng ä?
B: Lẽng, tẽin-ngìt/ hāo lẽng sōng.		 A: Was it cool the day before yesterday?
B: The day before yesterday was very nice.
5 A: Nï göi lāo-pû gäo mä?
B: Gäo, ngöi göi lāo-pû ēin-dëm gäo.		 A: Is your wife tall?
B: Yes, my wife is quite tall.
6 A: Nï göi xäm-mīn-dōi āi mä?
B: M~-āi, ngöi göi xäm-mīn-dōi m~-āi.		 A: Is your son short?
B: No, my son is not short.
7 A: Nï göi xäm-mīn-dōi fĩ mä?
B: M~-fĩ, ngöi göi xäm-mīn-dōi m~-fĩ. Küi sëo äk-dài.		 A: Is your son fat?
B: No, my son is not fat. He is too skinny.
8 A: Nï göi xäm-mīn-dōi gī ài lū-yä?
B: Ngöi göi xäm-mīn-dōi hāo xäi.		 A: How old is your son?
B: My son is very young.
9 A: Nêk gïm-mân gëo m~-gëo yä?
B: Ngôi gïm-mân hāo gëo.		 A: Are you (plural) tired today?
B: Yes, we are very tired today.
10 A: Nï gïm-mân gök-āk gī-hâo/ yä?
B: Ngöi gïm-mân gök-āk hāo hāo. Nï nēh?
A: Ngöi gïm-mân gök-āk m~-hài gī hāo.		 A: How do you feel today?
B: I feel fine. How about you?
A: I don't feel well today.
