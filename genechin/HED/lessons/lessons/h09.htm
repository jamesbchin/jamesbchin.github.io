Dialogue
1 A: Võng Xëin-Säng, nĩ göi fü-ngĩn/ hāo mä?
B: Küi hāo hāo. Nĩ göi fü-ngĩn/ nēh?
A: Küi yèk hāo hāo.		 A: Mr. Wong, how is your wife?
B: She's fine. How's your wife?
A: She's also fine.
2 A: Võng Xëin-Säng hâi gī-hâo/ göi nãm-ngĩn/ ä?
B: Võng Xëin-Säng hâi hāo nãm-ngĩn/.		 A: What kind of person is Mr. Wong?
B: Mr. Wong is a good person.
3 A: Võng Fü-Ngĩn/ hâi gī-hâo/ göi nuī-ngĩn/ ä?
B: Võng Fü-Ngĩn/ hâi hāo lëng göi nuī-ngĩn/.		 A: What kind of person is Mrs. Wong?
B: Mrs. Wong is a beautiful woman.
4 A: Lī Xëin-Säng hâi m~-hâi hāo tüng-mẽin göi hòk-säng/?
B: Hâi, Lī Xëin-Säng hâi hāo tüng-mẽin göi hòk-säng/.		 A: Is Mr. Lee a very intelligent student?
B: Yes, Mr. Lee is a very intelligent student.
5 A: Lī Xëin-Säng hâi m~-hâi hāo bōn-xù göi nãm-ngĩn/ nēh?
B: Hâi, Lī Xëin-Säng yèk hâi hāo bōn-xù göi nãm-ngĩn/.		 A: Is Mr. Lee a very capable person?
B: Yes, Mr. Lee is also a very capable person.
6 A: Kôi gâ chëh hâi m~-hâi hāo gï göi ä?
B: Hâi, kôi gâ chëh hâi hāo gï göi.		 A: Is this car very expensive?
B: Yes, this car is very expensive.
7 A: Nêin gâ chëh hâi m~-hâi hāo gï göi ä?
B: Hâi, nêin gâ chëh yèk hâi hāo gï göi.		 A: Is that car very expensive?
B: Yes, that car is also very expensive.
8 A: Kôi gâ chëh hâi m~-hâi hāo pẽng göi ä?
B: M~-hâi, kôi gâ chëh m~-hâi hāo pẽng göi.		 A: Is this car very inexpensive?
B: No, this car is not inexpensive.
9 A: Nêin gâ chëh hâi m~-hâi hāo pẽng göi nēh?
B: M~-hâi, nêin gâ chëh yèk m~-hâi hāo pẽng göi.		 A: Is that car very inexpensive?
B: No, that car is also not inexpensive.
10 A: Uï-m~-jì, uï-m~-jì.
B: M~-gīn-yël, m~-gīn-yël.		 A: I'm sorry.
B: That's allright.
