14 冖 2 冖 mìt mì Kangxi radical 14, cover; (<old>=幂[冪] mìt mì) cover-cloth, cover with cloth.⁸ "cover" radical in Chinese characters (Kangxi radical 14), occurring in 军, 写, 冠, etc.; It is called 秃宝盖[禿寶蓋] tūk-bāo-gôi tū bǎogài or 平宝盖 [平寶蓋] pẽin-bāo-gôi píng bǎogài.¹⁰
<台> The component, 冖, is commonly called 帽仔冇点 [帽仔冇點] mào-dōi-mäo-ēm.
