192 鬯 10 鬯 chēng chàng Kangxi radical 192; sacrificial wine; unhindered.⁸ wine used at ancient sacrificial ceremonies;⁶ <wr.> (=畅[暢] chēng chàng)⁶  smooth; free.
匕鬯不惊[匕鬯不驚] bï-chēng-būt-gëin bǐchàngbùjīng strict military discipline.¹  sacrificial offerings to ancestors at the ancestral shrines must not be disturbed.⁶
衅鬯[釁鬯] hïm-chēng xìnchàng fragrant wine for anointing a dead body.⁷
