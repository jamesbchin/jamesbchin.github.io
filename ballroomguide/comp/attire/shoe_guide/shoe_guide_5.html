What Type of Heels are there?

Besides the different height in heels, both men and women's dance shoes often have differently shaped heels. These heels do feel different when dancing, and it is up to you to decide which style fits you best. For beginners, it may be better to chose the lower heel, as these are easier to dance in and put less strain on the body. Higher heels in Latin allow for a nicer foot point and a longer leg line.
