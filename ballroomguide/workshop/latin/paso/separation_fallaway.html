Fallaway Ending to Separation (Bronze)

Precede this figure with the Separation, leading Lady forward to R Side over steps 5-8 and regaining hold with R hand

Man Foot Position Alignment Turn Foot-
work		 Lead & Shape Count
1 RF fwd, OP Commence facing LOD and turn to face wall 1/4 to R between preceding step and 1 HF or BF Slight CPP Shape 1
2 LF fwd, OP Facing against LOD 1/4 to R between 1&2 HF or BF " 2
3 RF back in Fallaway Position, R side leading Down LOD, backing DW 1/8 to R between 2&3 BH Achieve PP Shape 3
4 LF back in CBMP and Fallaway Position " No turn BF Maintain PP Shape 4
5 RF to side Facing centre 1/8 to R between 4&5 B or BF Return to normal hold 5
6 Close LF to RF " No turn B or BF Weight change 6
7 RF to side " " B or BF " 7
8 Close LF to RF " " B or BF " 8
Lady Foot Position Alignment Turn Footwork Count
1 LF fwd, OP Commence facing against LOD and turn to face centre 1/4 to R between preceding step and 1 HF or BF 1
2 RF fwd, OP Facing LOD 1/4 to R between 1&2 HF or BF 2
3 LF back in Fallaway Position, L side leading Down LOD, backing DC 3/8 to R between 2&3 BF 3
4 RF back in CBMP and Fallaway Position " No turn BF 4
5 LF to side Backing centre 1/8 to L between 4&5 B or BF 5
6 Close RF to LF " No turn B or BF 6
7 LF to side " " B or BF 7
8 Close RF to LF " " B or BF 8

Notes to Man and Lady

1 Other commencing alignments may be used depending on the alignment of the preceding Separation
2 Elevation may be used on 5-8, rising high on toes

Preceding Figures

Bronze Separation

Following Figures

Bronze-Gold Any syllabus figure commenced with feet together, depending on alignment

Suggested Styling

Silver Gradually attain slight CPP Shape over 5-8 of the preceding Separation

Alternative Styling for Steps 5-8

a Lower joined hands to upper thigh on 5, then return to normal hold, keeping hands close to body over 6-8
b Maintain Promenade shape over 5-8
c Achieve Counter Promenade shape on 5 and maintain Counter Promenade shape over 6-8, or gradually achieve Counter Promenade shape over 5-8
