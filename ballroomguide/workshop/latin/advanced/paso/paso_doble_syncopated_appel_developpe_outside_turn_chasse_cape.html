Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count

3 Leading Lady forward for 2 steps, moving to L side to end across front of body Turning to L, transfer full weight to LF, with Lady moving forward in Open PP 1/8 to L, facing DW B, foot flat (LF), i/e of T (RF) 2
