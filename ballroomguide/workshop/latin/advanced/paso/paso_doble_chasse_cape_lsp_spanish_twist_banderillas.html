Chasse Cape Action in Solo LSP - Spanish Twist - Check to Banderillas Shape

Commence facing wall in Left Side Position without hold throughout, both on RF, Upper Body Shape depending on previous figure

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-3 With Man following Lady's Shape LF to side into a Chasse Cape action in LSP, on same foot without hold. On 3, raise RF sideways to knee height as an Attitude Complete 1/8 to L, to end facing DW BH, B, BH (LF) & RF off floor 8&1
4 With Man following Lady's Shape RF crosses in front of LF with split weight into flexed knees in TP with R Sway 1/8 to L, facing LOD BH 2
5 With Man following Lady's Shape Untwist to L to end with weight on RF in TP with Man in front, straightening Sway 1/2 to L, facing against LOD B, foot flat (RF) & B (LF) 3
6 With Man following Lady's Shape LF closes to RF without weight in LSP, with no Sway, rising strongly through both feet 1/8 to L, facing DW against LOD T of both feet 4
7 With Man following Lady's Shape LF forward in LSP, without hold into strongly flexed knees. Check No turn, facing DW against LOD BH (LF) & pressure on B (RF) 5
8 With Man following Lady's Shape Transfer weight back to RF to normal height in LSP No turn, backing DC BH 6
9 With Man following Lady's Shape LF back and then Ronde RF outward and back without weight in LSP No turn, backing DC, RF pointing DC against LOD BH (LF) & RF off floor 7
10 Leading Lady visually RF back & across in LSP No turn, backing DC BH 8
11 With Man following Lady's shape Turning to L on RF, LF forward to TP without hold No turn, facing DW T of both feet &
12 Lady continuing to turn to L RF closes to LF with split weight, rising strongly through both feet in Open Position without hold. Banderillas shape No turn, facing DW T of both feet 1
