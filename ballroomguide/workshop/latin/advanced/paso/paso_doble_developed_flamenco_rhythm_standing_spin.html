Developed Flamenco Rhythm to Standing Spin

Commence facing DW in Solo RSP, with Upper Body Shape to R

Man/Lady Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Lady following Man Hold position on LF, tap B of RF in place sharply on floor in RSP No turn, facing DW LF (LF), B (RF) without weight 1
2 Lady following Man Hold position on LF, tap H of RF, slightly forward of LF, sharply on floor in RSP. Latin Cross   WF (LF), H (RF) without weight &
3 Lady following Man RF in front of LF, lowering sharply into a slightly flexed R knee in RSP. Latin Cross No turn, facing DW H flat 2
4 Visually aware Turning to R, LF sharply to side with split weight in RSP 1/8 to R, facing wall B &
5 Man following Lady Sharply replace weight to RF in RSP No turn, facing wall, RF pointing DW against LOD BH 3
6 Visually aware Turning to R, LF closes to RF without weight, rising, with both hands high above head in RSP 1/8 to R, facing DW against LOD, upper body facing wall T (both feet) 4
7 Man following Lady Lowering sharply, LF forward & across, small step, in RSP, lowering arms. Latin Cross No turn, facing DW against LOD H flat 5
8 Man following Lady Replace weight sharply back to RF in RSP No turn BH 6
9 Lady following Man Turning to L, LF to side in RSP 1/4 to R, facing DW BH 7
10 Lady following Man RF forward & across, small step, in RSP No turn, facing DW H flat &
11 Lady following Man Turning to R on RF in place, raise LF to side with a curved leg line, LF above R knee height, in RSP. Attitude 1/4 to R, facing DW against LOD, L knee pointing to wall, LF pointing LOD B, foot flat (RF), LF off floor 8
12 Man following Lady Maintain height of L knee, moving leg slowly across body to R, with LF pointing down in RSP No turn, facing DW against LOD WF (RF), LF off floor 1 2
13 Man following Lady LF forward & across, small step, in RSP No turn, facing DW against LOD BH 3
14 Visually aware Standing Spin to R on LF to end RF closes to LF without weight in RSP 7/8 to R on LF to end facing wall B, foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 4
