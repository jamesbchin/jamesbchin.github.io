Twists Action Opening to PP to end in Closed RSP

Commence facing DW in Closed Hold, with Upper Body Shape to L

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady back RF forward OP in Closed Hold No turn, facing DW HB 1
2 Leading lady to side in PP LF to side in PP No turn, moving along LOD, facing DW HB 2
3 Leading Lady forward in PP RF forward & across in PP No turn, moving along LOD, facing DW HB 3
4 Leading Lady forward Turning to R, LF back & slightly to side with R Sway in Closed Hold 3/8 to R, backing LOD BH 4
5 Leading Lady forward to R side RF corsses behind LF with split weight & R Sway in Closed Hold No turn, backing LOD B &
6 Leading lady forward OP Untwist to R, to end with weight on LF, side & slightly back, small step, with L Sway in Closed Hold 3/8 to R, facing DC B, foot flat (LF) & B (RF) 5
7 Leading lady back Turning to R, RF forward, OP, in Closed Hold 1/8 to R, facing LOD HB 6
8 Leading Lady to PP Turning to R, LF to side in PP 1/8 to R, facing DW BH &
9 Leading Lady forward in PP to turn to L Turning to L, RF forward & across in PP, T turned in 1/8 to L, facing LOD BH 7
10 Leading Lady forward to turn to L to Closed RSP Turning to L, LF closes to RF in Closed RSP, Lady facing opposite direction 3/8 to L, backing DW B, foot flat (RF) & BH (LF) 8

Commence backing DW in Closed Hold, with Upper Body Shape to R

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 LF back with Man OP No turn, backing DW BH 1
2 Turning to R, RF to side in PP 1/4 to R, facing DC BH 2
3 LF forward & across in PP No turn, facing DC HB 3
4 Turning to R, RF forward between Man's feet with L Sway in Closed Hold 1/8 to R, facing LOD HB 4
5 Turning to R, LF diagonally forward, passing Man's R side with L Sway in Closed Hol 1/8 to R, facing DW BH &
6 Turning to R, RF forward & across, OP, small step, R Sway in Closed Hold 1/4 to R, facing DW against LOD BH 5
7 Turning to R, LF back with man OP, in Closed Hold 1/8 to R, backing LOD BH 6
8 Turning to R, RF to side in PP 3/8 to R, facing DC BH &
9 Turning to L, LF forward in PP 1/8 to L, facing centre BH 7
10 Turning to L, RF closes to LF in Closed RSP, facing opposite direction 5/8 to L, facing DW B, foot flat (LF) & BH (RF) 8
