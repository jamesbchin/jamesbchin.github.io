Appel to PP - Quick Lock in PP - Forward Lunge in PP

Commence facing wall, in Closed Hold with Upper Body Shape to L

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady to Appel Turning to L, RF Appel to PP 1/8 to L, facing DW HF 1
2 Leading lady to side in PP LF to side in PP No turn, moving along LOD, facing DW HB 2
3 Leading Lady forward in PP Turning to L, RF forward, small step, in PP 1/8 to L, facing LOD B 3
4 Leading Lady forward to turn to L Turning to R, LF forward, small step, rising to T in PP 1/8 to R, facing DW, LF pointing & moving down LOD T &
5 Leading Lady to cross behind RF crosses behind LF in PP, on toes, T to H. Latin Cross No turn, facing DW T 4
6 Leading Lady forward in PP LF forward, small step, lowering in PP No turn, facing DW, LF pointing & moving along LOD T(H) &
7-8 Leading Lady forward to turn to P in PP Turning to L, RF forward in PP into a flexed R knee, L leg extended back. Joined L to R hands at chest height.
Hold position, Forward Lunge		 1/8 to L, facing LOD BH (RF) & i/e of T (LF) 5

67
9 Leading Lady to turn to L Turning to R, LF closes to RF in Contact Hold with joined L hand lowered to L side of L hip 1/4 to R, facing wall B, foot flat (RF) & BH (LF) 8

Commence backing wall in Closed hold with Upper Body Shape to R

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Turning to R, LF Appel to PP 1/8 to R, facing DC HF 1
2 RF to side in PP No turn, moving along LOD, facing DC HB 2
3 Turning to R, LF forward, small step, in PP 1/8 to R, facing LOD B 3
4 Turning to L, RF forward, rising to T, in PP 1/8 to L, facing DC, RF pointing & moving down LOD T &
5 LF crosses behind RF in PP, on toes, T to H. Latin Cross No turn, facing DC T 4
6 RF forward, lowering in PP No turn, facing DC, RF pointing & moving along LOD T(H) &
7-8 Turning to R, LF forward in PP into a flexed L knee, R leg extended back.
Hold Position. Forward Lunge		 1/8 to R, facing LOD BH (LF) & i/e of T (RF) 5
67
9 Turning to L, RF closes to LF in Contact Hold 1/4 to L, facing LOD B, foot flat (LF) & BH (RF) 8
