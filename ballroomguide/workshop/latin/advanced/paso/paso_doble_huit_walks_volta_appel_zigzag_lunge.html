Huit (Bota Fogo) Actions - Contra Walks & Volta Actions - Double Appel - Zigzag - Forward Lunge

Commence facing DW against LOD in Open L Contra Position without hold

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-3 Leading Lady visually LF forward in Open L Contra Position into a Huit action, LRL Complete 1/4 to L to end facing DW BH, B, BH 12&
4-6 Leading Lady visually RF forward in Open R Contra Position into a Huit action, RLR Complete 1/4 to R to end facing DW against LOD HB, HB 34&
7-8 Leading Lady visually LF forward in Open L Contra Position into 2 Circular Walks, LR Completing 3/8 to L to end facing LOD HB, HB 5678
9-13 Leading Lady visually LF in front of RF, H to T, as a Latin Cross in Open Contra Position into a Circular Volta Action, LRLRL Completing 1/2 to L to end backing LOD BH, B, BH, B, BH 1&2&3
14 Leading Lady visually, end with L to R hand hold RF closes to LF without weight, Double Appel in Open Position, end with weight on LF. Foot Change No turn, backing LOD H flat, both feet 4
15-17 Leading Lady forward into a Zigzag to take R hand hold RF back into a Zigzag, RLR, to end RF forward OP in Closed Hold Complete 3/8 to L to end facing DW BH, B, BH 5&6
18 Leading Lady to turn to R in place Turning to L, LF closes to RF in PP 1/8 to L, facing LOD BH &
19-20 Leading Lady forward, releasing hold with L hand RF forward in Open PP into a flexed R knee, L leg extended. Forward Lunge. Hold Position No turn, facing LOD BH (RF), i/e of T (LF) 78

Commence facing DC in Open L Contra Position without hold

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-3 LF forward in Open L Contra Position into a Bota Fogo action, LRL Complete 1/4 to L to end facing DC against LOD BH, B, BH 12&
4-6 RF forward in Open R Contra Position into a Huit action, RLR Complete 1/4 to R to end facing DC BH, B, BH 34&
7-8 LF forward in Open L Contra Position into 2 Circular Walks, LR Completing 3/8 to L to end facing against LOD HB, HB 5678
9-13 LF in front of RF, H to T, as a Latin Cross in Open L Contra Position, into a Circular Volta action, LRLRL 1/2 to L, facing LOD BH, B, BH, B, BH 1&2&3
14 RF closes to LF, Double Appel in Open Position to end with weight on RF No turn, facing LOD H flat, both feet 4
15-17 LF forward into a Zigzag, LRL, to end LF back with Man OP, taking Closed Hold Complete 3/8 to L to end backing DW BH, B, BH 5&6
18 RF closes to LF in PP 3/8 to R to end facing LOD BH &
19-20 LF forward in Open PP, into a flexed L knee, R leg extended. Forward Lunge. Hold Position No turn, facing LOD BH (LF), i/e of T (RF) 78

Suggested Popular Combination

Precede

 Any suitable figure commenced in Open Position (with Foot Change)

Follow

 Slip Appel into a Fallaway Reverse Turn

Suggested Advanced Combination

Precede

 Natural Spin (overturned to face wall with Foot Change)

Follow

 Transfer weight back in FP, count &, into Slip Appel - Reverse Swivel - Basic - Check - Chasse Cape Action
