Travelling Volta with Split Rhythm Action

Commence facing DW in Open Position with L to R hand hold, ready to move DC

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady sideways to R RF in front of LF in Open Position. Latin Cross No turn, facing DW, moving DC BH 1
2 Leading Lady sideways to R LF to side with split weight in Open Position No turn, facing DW, moving DC B a
3 Leading Lady sideways to R RF in front of LF in Open Position. Latin Cross No turn, facing DW, moving DC BH 2
4 Leading Lady sideways to R LF to side with split weight in Open Position, L knee straight No turn, facing DW, moving DC T a
5 Leading Lady to replace weight sideways to LF Replace split weight sideways to RF in Open Position, body centred, R knee straight No turn, facing DW, moving DW against LOD T 1
6 Leading Lady to remain in place LF behind RF in Open Position. High Latin Cross No turn, facing DW, moving DW against LOD T a
7 Leading Lady sideways to R RF forward and across LF in Open Position No turn, facing DW, moving DC BH 2

Commence backing DW in Open Position, ready to move DC

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 LF in front of RF in Open Position No turn, backing DW, moving DC BH 1
2 RF to side with split weight in Open Position No turn, backing DW, moving DC B a
3 LF in front of RF in Open Position No turn, backing DW, moving DC BH 2
4 RF to side with split weight in Open Position. R knee straight No turn, backing DW, moving DC T a
5 Replace split weight sideways to LF with body centred in Open Position, L knee straight No turn, backing DW, moving DW against LOD T 1
6 RF behind LF in Open Position. High Latin Cross No turn, backing DW, moving DW against LOD T a
7 LF forward and across in Open Position No turn, backing DW, moving DC BH 2

Notes

This figure may be danced in Right Side, Left Side, or Shadow Position, both dancing the Man's steps, or both dancing the Lady's
