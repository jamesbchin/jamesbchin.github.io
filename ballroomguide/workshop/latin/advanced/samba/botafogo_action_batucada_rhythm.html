Bota Fogo Action with Batucada Rhythm

Commence facing DW in Open Position without hold. Step 1 is danced on the last quarter of the preceding beat.

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Being visually aware of each other LF closes to RF No turn, facing DW B a
2 Being visually aware of each other Extend RF to side with split weight, R hip raised. Maintain centreline of body and head over LF No turn, facing DW B 1
3 Being visually aware of each other Replace full weight to LF No turn, facing DW B &
4 Being visually aware of each other RF closes to LF No turn, facing DW B a
5 Being visually aware of each other Extend LF to side with split weight, L hip raised. Maintain centreline of body and head over RF No turn, facing DW B 2
6 Being visually aware of each other Replace full weight to RF No turn, facing DW B &
7-12 Repeat steps 1-6        

Commence backing DW in Open Position without hold. Step 1 is danced on the last quarter of the preceding beat.

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 RF closes to LF No turn, backing DW B a
2 Extend LF to side with split weight, L hip raised. Maintain centreline of body and head over RF No turn, backing DW B 1
3 Replace full weight to RF No turn, backing DW B &
4 LF closes to RF No turn, backing DW B a
5 Extend RF to side with split weight, R hip raised. Maintain centreline of body and head over foot No turn, backing DW B 2
6 Replace full weight to LF No turn, backing DW B &
7-12 Replace steps 1-6      

Notes

Man and Lady may dance the same steps in L Side, R Side, or Tandem Position without hold. Alternative alignments are possible when solo.
