Box Movement

Commence facing LOD in Open CPP

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady forward & across, then releasing hold with L hand RF back & across, with Lady commencing to move across front of body to R side No turn, backing against LOD BH 1
2 Taking hold with both hands on Lady's hips, lead her sideways to R side LF to side with lady moving across front of body to R side No turn, facing LOD B a
3 Leading lady back, then releasing hold RF forward in RSP, without hold, slightly forward of Lady No turn, facing LOD BH 2
4 With a visual connection LF forward & across, commencing to move to R side in front of Lady No turn, facing LOD, with slight upper body turn to R BH 1
5 Lady taking hold with both hands on Man's hips, leading him sideways to R side RF to side, moving across front of Lady's body to R side No turn, facing LOD, with slight upper body turn to L B a
6 With a visual connection LF back in LSP, without hold, slightly back of Lady No turn, backing against LOD BH 2
7-9 With a visual connection Repeat steps 1-3, without hold, to end in Tandem Position No turn, facing LOD BH, B, BH 1a2
10-12 With a visual connection, to end with hold required for the following figure LF forward into a Cruzados Lock to end in the position required for the following figure No turn, facing LOD BH, B, BH 1a2

Commence facing LOD in Open CPP

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 LF forward & across, commencing to move to R side in front of Man No turn, facing LOD, with slight upper body turn to R BH 1
2 RF to side, moving across front of Man's body to R side No turn, facing LOD, with slight upper body turn to L B a
3 LF back in RSP, without hold, slightly behind Man No turn, backing against LOD BH 2
4 RF back & across, with Man commencing to move across front of body to R side No turn, facing LOD BH 1
5 LF to side, with Man moving across front of body to R side No turn, facing LOD B a
6 RF forward in LSP, without hold, slightly forward of Man No turn, facing LOD BH 2
7-10 LF forward into a Four Step Turn to L, LRLR, to end on RF, Spiral Action, in TP without hold 2 turns to L, to end facing LOD B, B, B, BH a1a2
11-13 LF forward into a Cruzados Lock to end in the position required for the following figure No turn, facing LOD B, B, BH 1a2

Suggested Popular Combination

Precede

 Promenade and Counter Promenade Runs

Follow

 Cruzados Walks and Locks

Suggested Advanced Combination

Precede

 Shadow Rocks to end in Open CPP

Follow

 Having ended in Shadow Position, Advanced Maxixe
