Sliding Doors Development

Commence facing wall, having danced an Alemana or other movement, to end with RF fwd and slight across LF toward Lady's right side

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady to turn to R, then releasing hold LF fwd and slight across in front of RF in RSP. Checked Forward Walk No turn, facing wall, upper body turn to R BH (LF), pressure on B (RF) 2
2 Leading Lady visually Transfer weight backward to RF in RSP No turn, backing centre, upper body turning to L BH 3
3 Leading Lady visually LF crosses behind RF, Pressed Backward Walk. Lady's L hip at nearly a 90° angle to front of Man. Latin Cross. No turn, backing centre, LF backing DC against LOD B 4 1
4 Being visually aware of Lady RF to side into a flexed R knee, with L leg extended in a Lunge Line and R Sway. Lady in front of L side No turn, facing wall, upper body turning to L, RF pointing DW against LOD, LF pointing DW BH (RF), WF (LF) 2
5 Leading Lady visually Transfer weight sideways to LF, regaining normal standing height, Lady's R hip at nearly a 90° angle to front of Man No turn, facing wall, upper body turning to R WF 3
6 Leading Lady visually RF forward & slightly across in front of LF in RSP No turn, facing wall BH 4 1

Commence facing centre, having danced an Alemana or other movement to end LF forward toward Man's right side

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Turning to R on LF at end of previous beat, RF back in RSP. Opening Out action 1/2 turn to R on LF, backing centre B, foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 2
2 Transfer weight forward to LF in RSP No turn, facing wall BH 3
3 Turning to L, RF forward and across, small step, Pressed Forward Walk, moving across in front of Man toward his L side 1/8 to L on LF, facing DW, upper body overturned toward Man B, foot flat (LF), B(H) (RF) 4 1
4 Turning to R, LF forward to end backward in front of and on Man's L side. Forward Walk Turning. 1/2 to R, facing DC against LOD, upper body facing against LOD BH 2
5 Transfer weight forward to RF No turn, facing DC against LOD, upper body facing against LOD WF 3
6 LF forward and slightly across on Man's R side No turn, facing DC against LOD, upper body turned toward Man BH 4 1
