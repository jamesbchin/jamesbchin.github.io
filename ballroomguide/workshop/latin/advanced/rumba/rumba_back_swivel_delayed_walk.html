Rumba: Syncopated Backward Swivel Basic to Delayed Walk

Commence facing DW against LOD, in Open Position, with L to R hand hold, retracted at Lady's hip height

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady bwd, then to turn to L LF fwd in Open Position to end in Open TP. Checked Fwd Walk No turn, facing DW against LOD BH (LF), pressure on B (RF) 2
2 Leading Lady bwd Transfer weight back to RF to end in Open TP No turn, backing DC BH 3
3 Leading Lady to close, then turn to R LF closes to RF, slightly back, in Open Position No turn, backing DC BH &
4 Leading Lady fwd RF back in Open Position No turn, backing DC BH 4 1
5 Leading Lady to hold position Slowly extend LF back without weight in Open Position. Extended Backward Walk No turn, backing DC WF (RF), i/e of T (LF) 2
6 Leading Lady fwd Transfer full weight bwd to LF in Open Position No turn, backing DC TH 3
7 Leading Lady fwd, taking R hand hold on Lady's back RF closes to LF in Closed Hold No turn, backing DC BH 4 1
8 Leading Lady fwd, to end to side and slightly fwd LF to side and slightly back in Contact Hold No turn, backing DC, slight upper body turn to R BH &

Commence backing DW against LOD, in Open Position

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 RF back, then turn to L on RF. Extend LF back without weight in TP, Extended Backward Walk No turn, backing DW against LOD, then 1/2 to L on RF at e/o 1, to end backing DC BH, then B, foot flat (RF), i/e of T (LF) 2
2 Transfer weight back to LF in Open TP No turn, backing DC T(H) 3
3 Close RF to LF, then turn to R on RF to Open Position 1/2 to R on RF, facing DC B with foot flat &
4 LF fwd in Open Position, then slightly flex L knee and raise RF bwd, approximately knee height No turn, facing DC BH (LF), RF off floor 4 1
5 Extend RF fwd off floor without weight in Open Position. Extended Forward Walk No turn, facing DC WF (LF), end o/e of T (RF) 2
6 Transfer full weight fwd to RF in Open Position No turn, facing DC BH 3
7 LF fwd, small step, in Closed Hold No turn, facing DC BH &
8 RF to side and slightly fwd in Contact Position No turn, facing DC, slight upper body turn to R BH 4 1

Suggested Popular Combination

Precede

 Any suitable figure ended in Open Position

Follow

 Cuban Rock

Suggested Advanced Combination

Precede

 Lady's Continuous Spins to L, to Open Position

Follow

 Cuban Rock into step 6 of High Ronde to R
