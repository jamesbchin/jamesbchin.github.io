Rumba: Overturned Slow Curl to Runaway - Check to Sit Line

Commence facing wall in Fan Position, with joined L arm extended to Lady's lower rib cage height

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Allowing Lady to close LF fwd as a Checked Fwd Walk in Fan Position No turn, facing wall BH (LF), pressure on B (RF) 2
2 Leading Lady fwd Transfer weight back to RF No turn, backing centre BH 3
3 Raising L hand, leading Lady fwd LF to side with split weight, Lady R side at a 90° angle to front of R side Facing wall, LF pointing DW, RF pointing DW against LOD T (LF), WF (RF) &
4 Leading Lady to turn to L, under arm Turning to R, slowly take full weight to LF and flex L knee into a Lunge Line, to end almost in TP Slowly turn body 1.8 to R toward Lady, LF pointing DW, RF pointing DW against LOD TH (LF), WF (RF) 41.2
5 Leading Lady fwd Transfer weight sideways to RF, straighten knees in TP No turn, facing DW against LOD WF 3
6 Leading Lady fwd LF fwd, small step in TP No turn, facing DW against LOD BH &
7 Leading Lady to turn to R, in place Rise to cross RF behind LF without weight in Closed Position No turn, facing DW against LOD T, both feet 4 1

Commence backing LOD in Fan Position

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 RF closes to LF in Fan Position No turn, backing LOD BH 2
2 LF fwd No turn, facing against LOD BH  3
3 RF fwd, moving across in front of Man to end with R side at a 90° angle to his R side No turn, facing against LOD BH &
4 Turning to L on RF, allow LF to cross in front of RF without weight to end almost in TP. High Spiral Cross 1 turn to L on RF to end with RF pointing against LOD, upper body underturned to end facing DC against LOD T (RF) btw 3-4, end o/e of T (LF) 41.2
5 Turning to L, LF fwd in TP. Runaway action 1/8 to L, facing DW against LOD, upper body remains underturned T(H) 3
6 RF fwd in TP. Runaway action No turn, facing DW against LOD BH &
7 Turning to R on RF in place, LF closes to RF, slightly forward without weight, in flexed knees to end in Closed Position. Sit Line (Closed) 1/2 to R on RF, backing DW against LOD B, foot flat (RF), i/e of T (LF) 4 1
