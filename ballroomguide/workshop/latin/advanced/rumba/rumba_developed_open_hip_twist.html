Developed Open Hip Twist

Commence facing DW against LOD in Open Position with joined L arm retracted at Lady's hip height

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady bwd LF fwd in Open Position. Checked Fwd Walk No turn, facing DW against LOD BH (LF), pressure on B (RF) 2
2 Leading Lady to trans weight fwd Transfer weight back to RF in Open Position No turn, backing DC BH 3
3 Leading Lady fwd LF to side with split weight, Lady moving toward R side 1/8 to L, facing wall, upper body under turned Slight pressure on i/e of B (LF), WF (RF) &
4 Leading Lady to turn to R Slowly transfer full weight to LF into a flexed L knee with R leg straight and L Sway, end with Lady's L hip at a 90° angle to front of R R side. Lunge Line No turn, facing wall, upper body to R. LF pointing DW, RF pointing DW against LOD BH (LF), WF (RF) 4 1
5 Leading Lady 2 steps fwd and then to turn to L under raised L hand Transfer weight sideways to RF to end with Lady's L hip at a 90° angle in front of body No turn, facing wall WF 2
6 Leading Lady 2 steps fwd, then to turn to L Transfer weight sideways to LF 1/8 to L, facing DW BH 3
7 Leading Lady bwd RF fwd to end in Open Position 1/8 turn to L, facing LOD BH 4 1

Commence backing DW against LOD in Open Position

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 RF back in Open Position No turn, backing DW against LOD BH 2
2 Transfer weight fwd to LF in Open Position No turn, facing DC BH 3
3 RF fwd moving toward man's R side No turn, facing DC BH &
4 Hold position, turning to R as a Hip Twist, to end with L hip at a 90° angle to front of Man's R side. Extend LF to side without weight 1/8 to R on RF facing LOD, upper body under turned toward man, LF pointing to centre B, foot flat (RF), i/e of T (LF) 4 1
5 LF fwd to pass across front of Man toward his L side No turn, facing LOD, moving DW BH 2
6 RF fwd and slightly across, then turn to L with a Spiral Action No turn, facing LOD, then 7/8 to L on RF, facing DW B (RF), o/e of T (LF) &
7 LF fwd to Man's L side 1/8 to L, facing LOD BH 3
8 RF fwd in Line with LF to end back in Open Position, Forward Walk Turning 1/2 to L to end backing LOD BH &
9 LF back in Open Position No turn, backing LOD BH 4 1

Other Possible Shapes

1 1-3 may be danced as steps 1-3 of a normal Open Hip Twist, followed by 5-9 as described above.
2 1-4 may be danced as described above, followed by the 4-6 of a normal Open Hip Twist.
3 1-4 may be danced as described above, followed by the Syncopated Spiral, led at hip height with step 9 turned to R. Described in the Telespin to lady's Spiral Turn to Fan Position.
9. The turn to right is the normal Forward Walk Turning; however, some dancers prefer to turn earlier on RF at the end of 8. This increases the speed of turn and weight change, providing additional time on step 9 to express the Lady's feelings and body language to the audience.
