Flicks in Open Position with Double and Single Rhythm - Close, Tap, Kick - Botafogo

Commence facing against LOD in Open Position with L to R and R to L hands in Low Double Hold

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-2 Leading Lady into 1-2 of a Link LF back in Open Position, into 1-2 of a Link, LR No turn, backing LOD BH, BH 1.2
3-4 Leading Lady to remain in place Hop on RF
