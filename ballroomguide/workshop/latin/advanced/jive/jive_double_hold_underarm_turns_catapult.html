Double Hold Underarm Turns - Catapult Ending

Commence facing LOD in Open Position in low Double Hold, R to R and L to L hand hold, with R hands on top

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-2 Leading Lady to turn to R under both arms LF in place to Mark Time, LR, to end in Open Position with joined L hands on top No turn, facing LOD BH, BH 1.2
3 Leading Lady to remain in place Turning to L under raised hands, LF forward, small step 1/4 to L on RF, facing centre B, foot flat (RF), BH (LF) 3
4 Leading Lady to remain in place Continue to turn L under raised arms, RF to side, small step in Open Position with joined R hands on top 3/4 turn to L, facing LOD BH 4
5-6 Leading Lady to turn to R under both arms, repeat steps 1-2       1.2
7 Leading lady to remain in place Turning to L under raised hands, LF forward, small step 1/4 to L on RF, facing centre B, foot flat (RF), BH (LF) 3
8 Leading Lady to remain in place Continue to turn L under raised arms, RF to side, small step in TP Man in front with Lady slightly on L side 1/4 turn to L, facing against LOD BH 4
9-10 Leading Lady backward into 1-2 of a Link LF forward into a flexed knee

Commence backing LOD in Open Position in low Double Hold, R to R and L to L hand hold, with R hands on top

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Turning to R under raised hands, RF forward, small step, with L side at a 90° angle to Man 1/4 to R on LF, facing centre B, foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 1
2 Continue turning to R under raised arms, LF to side, small step, in Open Position with joined L hands on top 3/4 to R, facing against LOD BH 2
3-4 RF in place to Mark Time, RL, to end in Open Position with joined hands on top No turn, facing against LOD BH 3.4
5-6 Turning under both arms to R, repeat steps 1-2     1.2
7-8 RF in place to Mark Time, to repeat steps 3-4 to end in TP, Man in front     3.4
9 RF back into a flexed R knee No turn, Backing LOD BH 1
10 Replace weight forward to LF, maintaining TP No turn, facing against LOD BH 2
11-13 RF forward, passing Man's L side into a Three Step Turn, RLR, to end RF forward on L side & slightly in front of Man's body 1 turn to R, to end facing against LOD B, B, BH 3&4
14-16 LF to side into a Turning Chase, LRL, to end LF back in Open Position, taking required hold Completing 1/2 to R, backing against LOD B, B, BH 1a2
