Miami Special Entry - Circular Kick Ball Changes - Three Step Turn to Right

Commence facing against LOD in Open Position with R to R hand hold

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-2 Leading Lady back into 1-2 of a Link LF back into steps 1-2 of a Link No turn, backing LOD BH, BH 1.2
3-5 Leading Lady to turn L under raised R hand Turning to R, LF to side into a Turning Chasse, LRL, to end LF back in Closed LSP, placing Lady's R hand behind Man's neck on his R shoulder, then release hold with R hand 3/4 to R to end backing centre B, B, BH 3a4
6-8 Leading Lady forward, turning to R Placing L hand around Lady's back, Turning to R, Flick RF forward into a Flick Ball Change, almost in place, in Closed LSP 1/4 to R, facing against LOD WF (LF), RF off floor, B, BH 1a2
9-14 Continue leading Lady forward, turning to R, to repeat steps 6-8, the Flick Ball Change, twice in Closed LSP Complete a further 1/2 to R to end facing LOD   3a4
1a2
15-17 Leading Lady to turn to R and then to release hold RF crosses behind LF into an Alternative Coaster Step, RLR, to end RF forward in Open Position, taking L to R hand hold No turn, facing LOD B, B, BH 3a4

Commence backing against LOD in Open Position with R to R hand hold

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-2 RF back into steps 1-2 of a Change of Places L to R No turn, backing against LOD, then facing LOD BH, BH 1.2
3-5 Turning to L, RF to side into a Turning Chasse, RLR, to end RF back in a Closed LSP to end with R hand behind Man's neck on his R shoulder 3/4 to L, backing centre B, B, BH 3a4
6-8 Turning to R, Flick LF forward into a Travelling Flick Ball Change in Closed LSP to end RF forward, small step 1/4 to R, facing against LOD WF (RF), LF off floor, B, BH 1a2
9-14 Repeat steps 6-8, the Travelling Flick Ball Change, twice in Closed LSP Complete a further 1/2 to R to end facing LOD   3a4
1a2
15-17 Turning to R, LF to side, Spiral Action, into a Three Step Turn, LRL, to end LF back in Open Position 1.1/2 to R to end backing LOD B, B, BH 3a4
