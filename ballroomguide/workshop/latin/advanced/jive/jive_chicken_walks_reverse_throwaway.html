Chicken Walks - Reverse Throwaway Action

Commence facing LOD in Open Position with Low L to R hand hold

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading lady forward Turning to R on RF, LF forward & across in flexed L knee, L side at a 90° angle to front of Lady 3/8 to R on RF, facing DW against LOD, upper body turn less B, foot flat (RF), BH (LF) 1
2 Leading lady forward Swivel to L on LF, RF to side in flexed R knee, L side at a 90° angle to front of Lady 1/8 to L, facing wall, LF pointing DW B, foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 2
3 Leading lady forward to repeat step 1   1/8 to R on RF, facing DW against LOD, upper body turns less B, foot flat (RF), BH (LF) 3
4 Leading lady forward to repeat step 2   1/8 to L, facing wall, LF pointing DW B, foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 4
5 Leading Lady forward toward Man's L side Turning to R, LF back with lady moving toward L side of Man 1/4 to R, backing LOD B 1
6 Leading Lady forward to pass Man's L side RF closes to LF with Lady in LSP, facing same direction No turn, facing against LOD B &
7 Leading lady forward and then to turn L under raised L hand LF forward, to end with Lady in LSP, forward of Man's body No turn, facing against LOD BH 6
8-10 Leading Lady to Run forward, then to turn R on 10 RF crosses behind LF in TP, T to H, Latin Cross, to end RF forward in Open Position No turn, facing against LOD, slight upper body turn to R on 10 B, BH, BH 7&8
