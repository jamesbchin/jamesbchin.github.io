Single Beat Shoulder Spin

Commence facing against LOD in Open Position with R to R hand hold

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-2 Leading lady back into 1-2 of a Link LF back into 1-2 of a Link in Open Position with R to R hand hold No turn, backing LOD to end facing against LOD BH, BH 1.2
3  Leading Lady to turn to L under raised arm, then lower R arm behind back Turning to L, LF forward in TP with Man in front 1/8 to L, facing DW against LOD BH 3
4 Placing L hand on Lady's R shoulder Turning to L, RF to side, small step, in LSP 1/8 to L, facing wall BH 4
5 Leading Lady to turn to R, then releasing hold Turning to L, LF almost in place in Open Position 1/8 to L, facing DW BH 1
6 Taking required hold to check Lady's turn Turning to L, RF almost in place in Open Position 1/8 to L, facing LOD BH 2
7-9 Leading lady to maintain position Hold position on RF into a Hesitation Ball Change in Open Position, RLR No turn, facing LOD WF (RF), B, BH 3a4

Commence backing against LOD in Open Position with R to R hand hold

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1-2 RF back into 1-2 of a Link in Open Position No turn, backing against LOD to end facing LOD BH, BH 1.2
3  Turning to L, RF forward to end back in TP with Man in front. Loose Spiral Cross 1/8 to L, facing DC, then complete a further 1/2 to L on RF to end facing DW against LOD B(H)B 3
4 Turning to L, LF to side, small step, in LSO 1/8 to L, facing wall BH 4
5 Turning to R on LF, RF almost in place, then spin to R on RF to end in Open Position No turn, backing LOD BH 1
6 LF back in Open Position No turn, backing LOD BH 2
7-9 Hold position into a Hesitation Ball Change in Open Position No turn, backing LOD WF (LF), B, BH 3a4
