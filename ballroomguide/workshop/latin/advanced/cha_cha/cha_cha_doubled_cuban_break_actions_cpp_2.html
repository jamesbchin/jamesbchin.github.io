Doubled Cuban Break Actions from Open CPP (2)

Commence facing LOD in Closed Position with L to R hand hold

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady to turn to L & forward Turning to R, LF forward in Open CPP. Checked Forward Walk 1/4 to R, facing wall B, foot flat (RF), BH (LF) 2
2 Leading Lady to transfer weight backward Transfer split weight back to RF in Open Counter Fallaway Position No turn, backing centre B &
3 Leading Lady to transfer weight forward Transfer split weight forward to LF in Open CPP No turn, facing wall BH 3
4 Leading Lady to transfer weight backward Transfer full weight back to RF in Open Counter Fallaway Position No turn, backing centre B &
5-6 Leading Lady to transfer weight forward to repeat steps 3-4, to end RF back with full weight No turn to end backing centre BH, BH 4&
7 Leading Lady to turn to R Turning to L, LF to side in Closed Position 1/4 to L, facing LOD BH
1
8 Leading Lady to close RF closes to LF in Closed Position No turn, facing LOD BH &
9-12 Leading Lady to turn to L, turn to R, LF forward to repeat steps 1-4, end RF back with full weight in Open Counter Fallaway Position 2&3&
13-15 Leading Lady to turn to R & forward, then release hold Turn to L on RF, then LF forward into a Three Step Turn, LRL, to end to side in Closed Position 1/2 to L on RF, facing centre. Complete a further 3/4 to L to end facing LOD B, foot flat (RF), then B, B, BH 4&1

Commence backing LOD in Closed Position with L to R hand hold

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Turning to L, RF forward in Open CPP. Checked Forward Walk 1/4 to L, facing wall B, foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 2
2 Transfer split weight back to LF in Open Counter Fallaway Position No turn, backing centre B &
3 Transfer split weight forward to RF in Open CPP No turn, facing wall BH 3
4 Transfer weight back to LF in Open Counter Fallaway Position No turn, backing centre B &
5-6 Transfer split weight forward to RF in Open CPP to repeat steps 3-4 to end LF back with full weight, backing centre BH, BH 4&
7 Turning to R, RF to side in Closed Position 1/4 to R, backing LOD BH
1
8 LF closes to RF in Closed Position No turn, backing LOD BH &
9-12 Turning to L, RF forward to repeat steps 1-4 to end LF back with full weight in Open Counter Fallaway Position   2&3&
13-15 Turning to R on LF, then RF forward into a Three Step Turn, RLR, to end RF to side in Closed Position 1/2 to R on LF, facing centre. Complete a further 3/4 to end backing LOD B, foot flat (LF), then B, B, BH 4&1
