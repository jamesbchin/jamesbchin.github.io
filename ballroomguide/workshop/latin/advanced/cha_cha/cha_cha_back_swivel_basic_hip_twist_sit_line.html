Back Swivel Basic - Hip Twist Actions to Tandem Sit Line

Commence facing DW against LOD in Open Position, with joined L arm retracted at Lady's hip height

Man Lead Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 Leading Lady backward, then to turn to L LF forward in Open Position, Checked Forward Walk to end in TP No turn, facing DW against LOD BH (LF), pressure on B (RF) 2
2 Leading Lady backward, then to turn to R Transfer weight back to RF, to end in Open Position No turn, backing DC BH 3
3-5 Leading Lady forward across body to L side, taking R hand hold LF back into a Ronde Chasse to end with Lady at a 90° angle in front of body in Closed Hold 1/4 to L, facing DW B, Flat, BH 4&1
6 Leading Lady forward across body to L side Transfer split weight sideways to RF with Lady's hip at a 90° angle to front of L side No turn, facing DW, upper body to L toward lady WF (both feet) 2
7 Leading Lady to turn to L, then forward across body to R side Transfer weight sideways to LF with Lady's R hip at a 90° angle to front of R side No turn, facing DW, upper body to R toward Lady WF (both feet) 3
8-10 Leading Lady to turn to R in place, Release hold to end with both hands on Lady's hips Turning to R, split feet further apart to end with weight centred in Contact TP [6-10 Cuban Rock Action] 1/8 to R, facing wall WF (RF), i/e of T (LF) 4&1

Commence backing DW against LOD in Open Position

Lady Foot/Body Position Turn/Alignment Footwork Count
1 RF back, then turn to L on RF, extend LF backward without weight in TP. Extended Backward Walk No turn between previous step and 1, backing DW against LOD, then 1/2 to L on RF at end of 1 to end backing DC BH, then B with foot flat (RF), i/e of T (LF) 2
2 Transfer weight back to LF, then turn R on LF to Open Position as a Spiral Action No turn between 1-2, backing DC, then 1/2 to R on LF at end of 2 to end facing DC TH, then B with foot flat (LF), o/e of T (RF) 3
3-5 RF forward into a Forward Lock to end in front of Man with L side at a 90° angle in Closed Hold No turn, facing DC BH, B, BH 4&1
6 LF forward & slightly across No turn, facing DC, upper body turn to L BH 2
7 Turning to L on LF, RF forward & slightly across, with R side at a 90° angle in Closed Hold. Hip Twist Action 3/8 to L on LF at end of previous beat, facing against LOD, upper body turns less B with foot flat (LF), BH (RF) 3
8 Turning to R on RF, LF forward & slightly across, with L side at a 90° angle in Closed Hold. Hip Twist Action 3/8 to R on RF at end of previous beat, facing DC, upper body turns less B with foot flat (RF), BH (LF) 4
9 Turning to L on LF, RF forward & slightly across to repeat step 7     &
10 Turning to R on RF, LF closes to RF, slightly forward without weight and with both knees flexed in contact TP. Sit Line 7/8 to R on RF, facing DW against LOD, upper body facing wall B with foot flat (RF), i/e of T (LF) 1
