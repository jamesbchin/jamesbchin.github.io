Natural Turn and Back Lock (Bronze)

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF fwd Facing DW Com to turn R Rise e/o 1
2 LF to side Backing DC 1/4 between 1-2 Up
3 RF closes to LF Backing LOD 1/8 between 2-3 Up. Lower e/o 3
4 LF back Backing LOD - Com to rise e/o 4, NFR
5 RF back, R side leading Backing LOD - Cont to rise on 5 and 6
6 LF crosses in front of RF Backing LOD - -
7 RF diag back Backing LOD - Up. Lower e/o 7
8 LF back in CBMP Backing LOD - -
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T; 3 TH; 4 TH; 5 T; 6 T; 7 TH; 8 T
CBM on 1, 4, and 8
Sway SRRSSSSS
Timing SQQSQQSS
Note The Man may use foot rise on step 4. The Footwork would be THT, but L heel must not rise abruptly.
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF back Backing DW Com to turn R Rise e/o 1, NFR
2 RF to side Pointing LOD 3/8 between 1-2, body turns less Up
3 LF closes to RF Facing LOD Body completes turn Up. Lower e/o 3
4 RF fwd Facing LOD - Com to rise e/o 4
5 LF fwd, L side leading Facing LOD - Cont to rise on 5 and 6
6 RF crosses behind LF Facing LOD - -
7 LF diag fwd, preparing to step OP Facing LOD - Up. Lower e/o 7
8 RF fwd in CBMP, OP Facing LOD - -
Footwork 1 TH; 2 T; 3 TH; 4 HT; 5 T; 6 T; 7 TH; 8 H
CBM on 1, 4, and 8
Sway SLLSSSSS
Timing SQQSQQSS

Preceding Figures

Bronze-Gold Any figure which leaves the RF free to move forward

Following Figures

Bronze Closed Impetus - Tipple Chasse to R - Running Finish
Gold Tipsy to R
Note 1-3 Natural Turn may be danced to end backing DW of new LOD at corner to follow with Back Lock
