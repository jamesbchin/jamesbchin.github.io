Heel Pivot (Quarter Turn to Left) (PreBronze)

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF back Backing DC Com to turn L -
2 LF closes toward RF Toward alignment of 3 Cont to turn on R heel (Heel Pivot) Slight rise between 1-3, NFR
3 RF closes to LF slightly fwd without weight Facing DW 1/4 between 1-3 -
4 LF fwd Facing DW - -
Footwork 1 TH; 2 H; 3 H(RF) with pressure on T of LF; 4 H
CBM on 1 and 4
Sway SRRS
Timing SQQS
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF fwd Facing DC Com to turn L Rise e/o 1
2 RF to side Backing wall 1/8 between 1-2 Up
3 LF closes to RF Backing DW 1/8 between 2-3 Up. Lower e/o 3
4 RF back Backing DW - -
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T; 3 TH; 4 T
CBM on 1 and 4
Sway SLLS
Timing SQQS

Note

 3/8 turn may be made on this figure

Preceding Figures

Bronze Quarter Turn to R - Natural Spin Turn - Chasse Reverse Turn - Closed Impetus
Silver 1-3 Quick Open Reverse

Following Figures

Bronze Any Natural figure - Cross Chasse - Change of Direction - If Heel Pivot is ended DC after underturned natural Spin Turn or Closed Impetus: Chasse Reverse Turn, Progressive Chasse to R, or Double Reverse Spin
Silver From DC as above: Quick Open Reverse, or Closed Telemark
Gold Cross Swivel - Tipsy to R
