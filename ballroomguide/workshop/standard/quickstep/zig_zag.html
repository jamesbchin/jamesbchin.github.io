Zig-Zag, Back Lock, and Running Finish (Bronze)

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF fwd Facing LOD Com to turn L -
2 RF to side Backing wall 1/4 between 1-2 -
3 LF back in CBMP Backing DW 1/8 between 2-3, body turns less Com to rise e/o 3, NFR
4 RF back Backing DW - Cont to rise on 4 and 5
5 LF crosses in front of RF Backing DW - -
6 RF diag back Backing DW - Up. Lower e/o 6
7 LF back in CBMP Backing DW Com to turn R Rise e/o 7
8 RF to side and slightly fwd Pointing to LOD 3/8 between 7-8, body turns less Up
9 LF fwd, preparing to step OP, L side leading Facing LOD Body turn to R Up. Lower e/o 9
10 RF fwd in CBMP, OP Facing LOD - -
Footwork 1 HT; 2 TH; 3 TH; 4 T; 5 T; 6 TH; 7 T; 8 T; 9 TH; 10 H
CBM on 1, 3 (slight), 7, and 10
Sway SSSSSSSLLS
Timing SSSQQSQQSS (SQQS may be used on 7-10)
Note No "R side lead" is needed on steps 4 and 6 as the body is already in the correct position for the Back Lock when the "body turns less" between steps 2-3
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF back Backing LOD Com to turn L -
2 LF closes to RF (Heel Turn) Facing DW 3/8 between 1-2, body turns less -
3 RF fwd in CBMP, OP Facing DW - Com to rise e/o 3
4 LF diag fwd Facing DW - Cont to rise on 4 and 5
5 RF crosses behind LF Facing DW - -
6 LF diag fwd Facing DW - Up. Lower e/o 6
7 RF fwd in CBMP, OP Facing DW Com to turn R Rise e/o 7
8 LF to side Backing DC 1/4 between 7-8 Up
9 RF back, R side leading Backing LOD 1/8 between 8-9 Up. Lower e/o 9
10 LF back in CBMP Backing LOD - -
Footwork 1 TH; 2 H; 3 HT; 4 T; 5 T; 6 TH; 7 HT; 8 T; 9 TH; 10 T
CBM on 1, 3 (slight), 7, and 10
Sway SSSSSSSRRS
Timing SSSQQSQQSS (SQQS may be used on 7-10)

Preceding Figures

Bronze Heel Pivot - Reverse Pivot - Double Reverse Spin - the last step of the Natural Turn and Change of Direction could be used

Following Figures

Bronze Any Natural figure - Forward Lock
Silver Quick Open Reverse
