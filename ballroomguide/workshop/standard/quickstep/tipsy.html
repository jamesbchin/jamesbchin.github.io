Tipsy to Right (Gold)

Tipsy to Left (Gold)

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF to side (small step) According to preceding figure Nil or up to 3/8 to R or L (see note) Slight rise on 1 and 2
2 Move LF halfway toward RF     Lower e/o 2
3 RF to side (small step)     Down, with sharp flexing of the knees

Continue with 2-5 of Forward Lock, QQSS

Footwork 1 T; 2 T; 3 IE of T and IE of T of LF
CBM Nil
Sway SLL or SSR
Timing Q&Q
Beat Value 1/2, 1/2, 1
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF to side (small step) According to preceding Figure Nil or up to 3/8 to R or L (see note) Slight rise on 1 and 2
2 Move RF halfway toward LF     Lower e/o 2
3 LF to side (small step)     Down, with sharp flexing of the knees

Continue with 2-5 of Back Lock, QQSS

Footwork 1 T; 2 T; 3 IE of T and IE of T of RF
CBM Nil
Sway SRR or SSL
Timing Q&Q
Beat Value 1/2, 1/2, 1

Preceding Figures

Gold 1-4 Natural Turn - Back Lock - Progressive Chasse to R - Reverse Pivot or Double Reverse Spin followed by a step fwd on LF, S - Cross Swivel followed by a step back on LF, S - Tipsy to L - Hover Corte

Following Figures

Gold 2-5 Forward Lock - Tipsy to L
Note When a Tipsy is danced after a forward step on the LF, any turn made will be to L. When it is danced after a step back on LF, and turn made will be to R

Tipsy to Left (Gold)

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF to side, small step According to preceding Figure Nil or up to 3-8 to R Slight rise on 1 and 2
2 Move RF halfway toward LF     Lower e/o 2
3 LF to side (small step)     Down with sharp flexing of the knees
Footwork 1 T; 2 T; 3 IE of T and IE of T of LF
CBM Nil
Sway SLL or SSR
Timing Q&Q
Beat Value 1/2, 1/2, 1
Note Precede with a step forward on RF, OP in CBMP
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF to side (small step) According to preceding figure Nil or up to 3/8 to R Slight rise on 1 and 2
2 Move LF halfway toward RF     Lower e/o 2
3 RF to side (small step)     Down with sharp flexing of the knees
Footwork 1 T; 2 T; 3 IE of T and IE of T of RF
CBM Nil
Sway SRR or SSL
Timing Q&Q
Beat Value 1/2, 1/2, 1

Preceding Figures

Gold Any figure which leaves the RF free to move forward - 1-4 Tipple Chasse to R - Tipsy to R

Following Figures

Gold 2-5 Back Lock - 2-8 Tipple Chasse - 2-12 V6 - Tipsy to R
