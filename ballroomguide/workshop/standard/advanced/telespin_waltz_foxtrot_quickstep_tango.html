Telespin - Waltz, Foxtrot, Quickstep, Tango

Tango. The figure will be danced as below, but of course with Tango Technique and character. Be careful not to use Rise or Swing in the Telemark action, but place feet without a Swivelling action. The Open Heel Pivot and Reverse Pivot must also be well grounded.

Commence facing DC in Closed Position

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF forward Facing DC Com to turn L Rise e/o 1
2 RF to side Backing DW 1/4 between 1-2 Up
3 LF back & slightly to side Along LOD, backing DC 1/4 between 2-3 Lower on 3
4 Turn L on L heel, RF in place without weight. Open Heel Pivot (Same foot position) Pointing LOD 3/8 on 4. Body turns less No rise
5 Rise on LF in place Facing LOD Body continues to turn Rise e/o 5
6 RF back & slightly to side, then turn on RF. Open Toe Pivot Backing LOD, end on required alignment 1/2 between 5-6, then up to 1/2 on 6 Up. Lower e/o 6
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T(H); 3 TH; 4 H with foot flat (LF), i/e of T (RF); 5 T; 6 THT
CBM slightly on 1
Sway S L L S S S
Head Weight C, C to L, L C C C
Timing (W) 1 2 3 & 1 &
Timing (F,Q) S Q Q & Q Q
Turning (T) Q Q & Q Q &

Commence backing DC in Closed Position

Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF back Backing DC Com to turn L No rise
2 LF closes to RF. Heel Turn Facing almost LOD Just under 3/8 between 1-2 No rise
3 RF diag forward, R side leading Pointing LOD Just under 1/8 between 2-3. Body turns more No rise
4 LF forward in CBMP, slightly across front of Man, small step (Same foot position) Facing DC 1/8 between 3-4 Rise between 4-5
5 RF back & slightly to side Backing LOD 3/8 between 4-5  
6 Turn on RF, LF held in front of RF without weight, then LF forward in CBMP. Open Toe Pivot Facing LOD, then turn to required alignment 1/2 between 5-6, then up to 1/2 on 6 Up. Lower e/o 6
Footwork 1 TH; 2 HT; 3 TH; 4 T Flat T; 5 T; 6 TH
CBM on 1 and 4
Sway S S S S S S
Head Weight C C R R, R to C, C
Timing (W) 1 2 3 & 1 &
Timing (F,Q) S Q Q & Q Q
Turning (T) Q Q & Q Q &

Telespin from Promenade Position

This figure may commence from Promenade Position.
Man's steps: RF fwd in PP & CBMP, HT; then continue from step 3 above
Lady's steps: LF fwd & across in PP & CBMP, HT; then continue from step 3 above
Waltz timing: 1 2 & 3 &
Foxtrot timing: S Q & Q &
Quickstep timing: S Q Q Q Q
Tango timing: Q Q & Q Q

 4-6 Reverse Turn

Precede to PP

Follow
