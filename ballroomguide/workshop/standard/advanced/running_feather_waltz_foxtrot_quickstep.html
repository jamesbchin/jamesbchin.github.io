Running Feather - Waltz, Foxtrot, Quickstep

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall (W) Rise & Fall (F,Q)
1 RF forward in CBMP, OP Facing DW Com to turn R Com to rise e/o 1 Rise e/o 1
2 LF diag forward in PP Facing DW. Body toward alignment of 3 Body cont to turn Cont to rise on 2 and 3 Up
3 RF forward & across in PP & CBMP Moving DW, facing Wall 1/8 between 2-3 - Up
4 LF back and slightly to side. Cont to turn on LF. Open Toe Pivot Backing DW, end facing required alignment 3/8 between 3-4, then up to 1/2 on 4 Up. Lower e/o 4 Up. Lower e/o 4
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T; 3 T; 4 THT
CBM slightly on 1
Sway L R R, R to S
Head Weight L R R, R to C
Timing (W) 1 & 2 3 or 1 2 & 3
Timing (F) S & Q Q or S Q & Q
Timing (Q) S Q Q S or S & Q Q
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall (W) Rise & Fall (F,Q)
1 LF back in CBMP, with Man OP Backing DW Com to turn to R Com to rise on 1, NFR Rise on 1, NFR
2 RF to side in PP DW, pointing LOD 3/8 between 1-2. Body turns less Cont to rise on 2 and 3 Up
3 LF forward & across in PP & CBMP, toe turned in Pointing DW. Body facing LOD 1/8 between 2-3 - Up
4 RF forward between Man's feet, then cont to turn. Spin Action Facing DW, to end backing required alignment on following step Cont body turn between 3-4, then up to 1/2 between 4 and following step Up. Lower on following step Up. Lower on following step
Footwork 1 TH; 2 T; 3 T; 4 T
CBM on 1 and 4
Sway S, slightly L, slightly L, slightly L to S
Head Weight R, R to L, L, L to C
Timing (W) 1 & 2 3 or 1 2 & 3
Timing (F) S & Q Q or S Q & Q
Timing (Q) S Q Q S or S & Q Q

Running Feather from Promenade Position

This figure may be commenced in Promenade Position.
Man's steps: 1 RF forward in CBMP & PP (HT); 2 LF diag forward preparing to step OP (T); 3 RF forward OP in PP (T); 4 LF diag forward in PP, L side leading (THT)
Lady's steps: 1 LF forward & across in PP & CBMP (HT); 2 RF to side & slightly back (T); 3 LF back in CBMP with man OP (T); 4 RF to side in PP (T)
