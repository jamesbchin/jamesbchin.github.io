Right Lunge - Waltz, Foxtrot, Quickstep, Tango

Tango. The figure will be danced as below, but of course with Tango technique and character. Be careful not to rise or swing into 1-2. Normally, each step will be taken sharply and with a feeling of a "stop" at the end of 2. A sharp staccato action is used on 3, however, it is also possible to add beats to 3 to create a very slow and expressive change of shape into the Right Lunge.

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF forward to end to side and slightly forward, L leg remains extended to side without weight DW to end pointing DW, body ends facing LOD. LF pointing DC Body turn to L Lower into a flexed L knee on previous step. Down in a flexed R knee
2 Transfer weight back to LF, toe turned in Backing DC against LOD Body turn to R, then LF turns 1/4 to R between 1-2 Rise e/o 2
3 RF back in CBMP, toe turned in. Pivot DC of new LOD, backing DC against new LOD to end facing required alignment 1/4 to L between 2-3, then up to 1/4 on 3 Lower e/o 3
Footwork 1 i/e of TH (RF) & i/e of T (LF); 2 i/e of THT (LF) & i/e of T (RF), then H of LF; o/e of THT
CBM on 3
Sway L (from previous step to R), S S
Head Weight Slightly to L (from previous step to R), L C
Timing (W) 1 2 3 (other timings are acceptable)
Timing (F,Q) S Q Q (other timings are acceptable)
Timing (T) S Q Q or SS Q Q
Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF back to end to side, R leg remains extended to side without weight DW to end facing against LOD, RF pointing DC against LOD 1/8 to L between previous step and 1 Lower into a flexed R knee on previous step. Down in a flexed L knee
2 Transfer weight forward to RF Facing DC against LOD, RF remains on same line 1/8 to R between 1-2, between feet Rise e/o 2
3 LF forward in CBMP. Pivot Facing DC of new LOD to end backing required alignment Up to 1/2 to L on 3 Lower e/o 3
Footwork 1 i/e of TH (LF) & i/e of T (RF); i/e of THT (RF) & i/e of T (LF); TH
CBM on 3
Sway slightly L, S S
Head Weight R (from previous step), L C
Timing (W) 1 2 3 (other timings are acceptable)
Timing (F,Q) S Q Q (other timings are acceptable)
Timing (T) S Q Q or SS Q Q
