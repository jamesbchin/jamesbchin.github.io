Fallaway Whisk from Promenade Position - Waltz, Foxtrot, Quickstep, Tango

Tango. The figure will be danced as below, but of course with Tango technique and character. Be careful not to rise or swing, but place the feet without a Swivelling action. The Fallaway Whisk from Promenade Position must remain grounded.

Commence in PP, pointing DW, body facing wall

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall (W) Rise & Fall (F,Q)
1 RF forward in PP & CBMP Pointing DW, body facing wall Com to turn to R Com to rise e/o 1 Rise e/o 1
2 LF back & slightly to side Backing DW 1/2 between 1-2 Cont to rise Up
3 RF to side & slightly forward in Fallaway Position Pointing LOD 3/8 between 2-3. Body turns less Up Up
4 LF crosses behind RF in Fallaway Position, toe turned in Pointing DW, body facing wall 1/8 between 3-4. Body turns more Up. Lower e/o 4 Up. Lower e/o 4
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T; 3 T; 4 TH
CBM on 1
Sway S R S S
Head Weight Slightly L, R R, slightly L
Timing (W) 1 & 2 3 or 1 2 3 & or 1 2 & 3
Timing (F) S & Q Q or S Q Q & or S Q & Q
Timing (Q) S S Q Q or S & Q Q
Timing (T) Q Q Q Q

Commence in PP, pointing LOD

Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall (W) Rise & Fall (FQ)
1 LF forward & across in PP & CBMP Pointing DW, body facing LOD 1/8 turn to R between previous step and 1 Com to rise e/o 1 Rise e/o 1
2 RF forward Facing DW Body continues to turn Cont to rise Up
3 LF back and slightly to side in Fallaway Position Backing DW 1/2 between 1-3 Up Up
4 RF crosses behind LF in Fallaway Position Facing LOD 3/8 between 3-4 Up. Lower e/o 4 Up. Lower e/o 4
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T; 3 T; 4 TH
CBM on 2
Sway S, slightly L, S S
Head Weight R, R to C, L R
Timing (W) 1 & 2 3 or 1 2 3 & or 1 2 & 3
Timing (F) S & Q Q or S Q Q & or S Q & Q
Timing (Q) S S Q Q or S & Q Q
Timing (T) Q Q Q Q
