3 RF closes to LF Backing LOD 1/8 between 2-3 Lower e/o 3 Up. Lower e/o 3
4 LF back, toe turned in. Pivot Down LOD to end facing LOD 1/2 on 4 No rise No rise
5 RF forward in CBMP Facing LOD Cont to turn to R Rise e/o 5 Rise e/o 5
