Double Reverse Spin Overturned

Commence in Closed Position, facing LOD

Man Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 LF forward Facing LOD Com to turn L Rise e/o 1
2 RF to side, then Ronde LF slightly outward Backing DW 3/8 between 1-2 Up. Lower very slightly as Ronde is danced
3 LF closes to RF without weight. Toe Pivot Facing LOD 5/8 between 2-3 Regain full rise
4 LF forward in CBMP, very small step, then turn on LF. Open Toe Pivot Facing DC to end backing LOD 1/8 between 3-4, 3/8 on 4 Up. Lower e/o 4
Footwork 1 HT; 2 T (RF), i/e of T (LF); 3 T (RF), i/e of T (LF); 4 TH
CBM slightly on 1, on 4
Sway S, slightly L, slightly L, L (Quickstep: S S S S)
Head Weight C L L L
Timing (W) 1 2 3 &
Timing (F) S Q Q &
Timing (Q) S S Q Q or S Q Q Q

Commence in Closed Position, backing LOD

Lady Foot Position Alignment Amount of Turn Rise & Fall
1 RF back Backing LOD Com to turn L No rise
2 LF closes to RF. Heel Turn Facing almost LOD Just under 1/2 between 1-2 No rise
3 RF to side & slightly back Backing DW Just over 3/8 between 2-3 Rise between 2-3
4 LF crosses in front of RF Backing LOD 1/8 between 3-4 Up
5 RF diag back, very small step, then turn on RF. Open Toe Pivot Backing DC. End facing LOD 1/8 between 4-5, 3/8 on 5 Up. Lower e/o 5
Footwork 1 TH; 2 HT; 3 T; 4 T; 5 TH
CBM on 1 and 5
Sway S S S S S
Head Weight C, slightly L, L L L
Timing (W) 1 2 & 3 &
Timing (F) S Q & Q &
Timing (Q) S Q Q Q Q or S Q & Q Q
