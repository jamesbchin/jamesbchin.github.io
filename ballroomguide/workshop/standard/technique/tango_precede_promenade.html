Methods of Preceding any Promenade Figure

1 Bronze Progressive Link
2 Bronze Walk on RF and place LF to side without weight, turning Lady to PP, count '&'
3 Bronze Natural Twist Turn
4 Bronze Natural Promenade Turn
5 Bronze From Closed Finish, keep feet in place and turn lady to PP at end of the last step, count '&'
6 Bronze From Open Finish as no. 2 above
7 Silver Four Step
8 Silver Fallaway Promenade
9 Gold Fallaway Four Step
10 Gold Oversway endings 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7
11 Gold Chase and endings 1, 3 and 4
12 Gold Five Step
13 Gold Contra Check

*Lady - step 2, Rock Turn
Lady - step 3, Fallaway Promenade
