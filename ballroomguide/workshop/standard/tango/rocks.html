Rock Back on RF (Bronze)

Rock back on LF (Bronze)

Man Foot Position Alignment or Direction Footwork
1 RF back in CBMP Down LOD BH
2 Transfer weight fwd to LF in CBMP Against LOD H
3 RF back, small step, in CBMP Down LOD BH
CBM Nil
Timing QQS
Amount of Turn Nil
Lady Foot Position Alignment or Direction Footwork
1 LF fwd in CBMP Down LOD H
2 Transfer weight back to RF in CBMP Against LOD BH
3 LF fwd, small step, in CBMP Down LOD H
CBM Nil
Timing QQS
Amount of Turn Nil
Note When a RF Rock follows a LF Rock, the first step of the RF Rock will be down LOD. When a Closed Finish follows a LF Rock, the first step of the Closed Finish will be DC

Preceding Figures

Bronze Walk back on LF - LF Rock

Following Figures

Bronze Back Corte - LF Rock

Rock Back on LF (Bronze)

Man Foot Position Alignment or Direction Footwork
1 LF back, L side leading Down LOD IE of BH
2 Transfer weight fwd to RF, R side leading Against LOD H
3 LF back, small step, L side leading Down LOD IE of BH
CBM Nil
Timing QQS
Amount of Turn Nil
Lady Foot Position Alignment or Direction Footwork
1 RF fwd, R side leading Down LOD H
2 Transfer weight back to LF Against LOD IE of BH
3 RF fwd, small step, R side leading Down LOD H
CBM Nil
Timing QQS
Amount of Turn Nil

Preceding Figures

Bronze Closed or Open Promenade or any Closed or Open Finish - 1-4 Progressive Side Step Reverse Turn - Rock on RF - Natural Twist Turn overturned
Silver Promenade Link - Brush Tap

Following Figures

Bronze Closed or Open Finish - RF Rock
Note When following an Open Finish, the LF Rock will be danced in CBMP with Lady outside and up to 1/4 turn to R may be made
